<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>In 1832 Ernst went to Paris, where he lived for several years . During this period he formed an intimacy with Stephen Heller, which resulted in their charming joint compositions—the <b>Pensees fugitives</b> for piano and violin .
</p>
