<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>A concert report from 1842 is mentioned by Rowe (<i>Heinrich Wilhelm Ernst: virtuoso violinist</i>, p.96) ("the <i>Polonaise</i>, eventually published as op.17, was premiered at either the seventh or the eighth of these Berlin concerts. [RGM:1/5/42:197]")
</p><p>Published with all three accompaniments simultaneously.
</p>
