<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>According to Grove Music, this was "the first surviving play set entirely to music . . . the score of which is the earliest one printed with a figured bass."  RISM classifies it as an oratorio.
</p>
