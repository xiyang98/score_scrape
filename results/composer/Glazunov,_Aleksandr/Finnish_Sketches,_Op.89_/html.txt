<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>To clear up any confusion here, for the oboe parts, the 2nd oboe plays English horn plays in the Aus <i>Kalewala</i> only then takes oboe for the CortÃ¨ge solennel movement. In the Percussion parts, the snare drum is the tamburo part since most likely the part would have been played on that instrument in today's orchestra. -Sallen112
</p>
