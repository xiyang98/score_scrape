<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p><a href="/wiki/Category:Rimskaya-Korsakova,_Nadezhda" title="Category:Rimskaya-Korsakova, Nadezhda">Nadezhda Rimskaya-Korsakova</a> (1848–1919), an accomplished pianist, was also the wife of Glazunov's teacher, the composer <a href="/wiki/Category:Rimsky-Korsakov,_Nikolay" title="Category:Rimsky-Korsakov, Nikolay">Nikolay Rimsky-Korsakov</a> (1844–1908).
</p><p>1900 has also been given as a date of composition.  Evidence between them has not been given either way. - Schissel
