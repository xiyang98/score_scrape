<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Veranderung often also translated as "Variation", a possible tag. Has been recorded at least twice (on the labels Crystal Records and Antes Edition.)
</p><p>Published again, by the way, in 1943 -- <span class="plainlinks"><a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://anno.onb.ac.at/cgi-content/anno-buch?apm=0&amp;aid=1000001&amp;bd=0001943&amp;teil=0203&amp;seite=00000091&amp;zoom=1">Hofmeister's <i>Monatsbericht</i> (1943), p.91</a></span>.
</p>
