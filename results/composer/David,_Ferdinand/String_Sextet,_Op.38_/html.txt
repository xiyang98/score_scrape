<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>A notice January 1 1861 from Leipzig in <b>The Athenaeum</b>, no.1732, 5 January 1861, page 23, gives a positive review to performances of both this and Brahms&#8217;s 1st sextet at the Gewandhaus concerts (and notes a performance of Jadassohn&#8217;s 1st symphony also, among other works, during the preceding season.) So premiered not later than, say, December 1860.
</p>
