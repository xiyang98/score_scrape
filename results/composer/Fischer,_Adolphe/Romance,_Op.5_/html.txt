<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Piano solo arrangement followed in 1878 <span class="plainlinks"><a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://anno.onb.ac.at/cgi-content/anno-buch?apm=0&amp;aid=1000001&amp;bd=0001878&amp;teil=0203&amp;seite=00000111&amp;zoom=1">Hofmeister's <i>Monatsbericht</i> (1878), p.111</a></span> (by Fehnenberger); orchestral arrangement followed in 1883: <span class="plainlinks"><a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://anno.onb.ac.at/cgi-content/anno-buch?apm=0&amp;aid=1000001&amp;bd=0001883&amp;teil=0203&amp;seite=00000115&amp;zoom=1">Hofmeister's <i>Monatsbericht</i> (1883), p.115</a></span>.
</p><p>Manuscript score and parts (of the orchestral version) exist at the Fleisher Collection, Free Library of Philadelphia (or at least are listed in their catalog.)
</p>
