<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>This work probably shouldn't be tagged until a more complete listing of the accompanying instruments is found, or until it is confirmed that it was open instrumentation.  Grove Music describes it as "Musicke of Sundrie Kindes, 4vv, insts (London, 1607/R); songs ed. in EL, 1st ser., iii (1921, 2/1966); 2 lyra viol duets, ed. in MB, ix (1955, 2/1962), 205, 206".
</p>
