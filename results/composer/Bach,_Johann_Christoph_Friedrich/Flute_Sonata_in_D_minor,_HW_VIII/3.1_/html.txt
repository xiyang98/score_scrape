<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Recorded on the 2009 Barthold Kuijken/Demeyere CD (on Accent) <i>Flute Sonatas by the Bach Sons</i> (first movement given as "Allegretto" which, even though Mendelssohn among others also use "Allegretto non troppo", makes more sense than that... ("Not too fast, not too slow, but not too much so. O-kay. Whatever you say, JCF Bach. Just this -one- thing. Which way? 2nd movement as "Andante - Recitativo".)
</p>
