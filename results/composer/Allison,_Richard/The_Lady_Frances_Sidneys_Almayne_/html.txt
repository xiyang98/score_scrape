<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>According to Grove Music, this had a rather specific instrumentation:  "treble viol (or violin), flute, lute, cittern, bandora and bass viol."  A bandora is a type of lute.
</p>
