<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Grove Music lists Allison's consort music as being for lute, treble viol (or violin), flute (or recorder), lute cittern, bandora and bass viol unless otherwise stated", and there is no other designation for this work, though these instruments add up to 6 players and the <i>In Nomine</i> is a 5-part work.
</p>
