<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<ul><li>Grove Music gives an initial publication date of 1710. However, there are at least two early editions of the sonatas that are without date, and Grove does not give the publisher's name for the 1710 edition.  The Jeanne Roger edition (which combined these sonatas with 6 from Ignazio Sieber) is undated, as is the Cross edition.  It is not possible to say whether either of these are the 1710 edition. 
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Though the cover page says "flute", it is most likely that recorder is intended, (the range fits exactly to the alto recorder), though these would also be playable on the flute.
</li></ul>
