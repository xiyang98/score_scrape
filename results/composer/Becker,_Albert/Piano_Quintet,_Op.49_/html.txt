<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Probably? the same as the Becker E-flat quintet Op.42 advertised by <a href="/wiki/Augener" title="Augener">Augener</a> in the Nov.1 1887 Monthly Musical Record.
</p><p>Performed in first half 1889 by the Mendelssohn Quartet Club of St Louis.
</p><p>The Fleisher Collection copy (which they estimate to 1887) was actually probably published around 1903, one guesses, from the Kammermusik-Bibliothek Edition Numbers (851-853).
</p>
