<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<ul><li>This is probably the longest Ricercare written in the renaissance
</li><li>Original clefs:  Superius (G2), Altus (C2, could be soprano, mezzo-soprano, or alto), Tenor (F3, "low" Tenor or "high" Bassus: would be more of a Baritone), Bassus (F4)
</li></ul>
