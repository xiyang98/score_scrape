<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>2nd movement entitled "Menuettino" (a heading Hoffmeister used for movements in some other works), with an unlabeled middle section; slow movement has an interesting (looking?) minore in E<span class="music-symbol" style="font-family: Arial Unicode MS, Lucida Sans Unicode; font-size:110%">&#x266d;</span> minor.
</p><p>Opus 62 also applies to a concerto for viola in D major, apparently.
</p>
