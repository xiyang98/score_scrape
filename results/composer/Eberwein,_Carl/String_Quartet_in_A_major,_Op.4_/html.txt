<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Note that the genre (speaking loosely) of <i>quatuor brillant</i>, as cultivated by Spohr also, implies a leading, almost concertante first violin and more accompanimental other parts, and that seems to be the case here (2nd note... Spohr produced some very fine works in the genre, I gather. Am going through this presently with a view to typesetting at least part of it and it looks enjoyable enough- I intend to be descriptive, not prescriptive) (Schissel)
</p><p>Publication mentioned in 1819 AMZ.
</p>
