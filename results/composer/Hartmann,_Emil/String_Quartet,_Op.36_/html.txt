<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>May or may not be the quartet in the same key published as Op.37 by Leichssenring ca.1885. (That is to say, until a copy of the latter is acquired and the two are compared, it's hard to say.)
