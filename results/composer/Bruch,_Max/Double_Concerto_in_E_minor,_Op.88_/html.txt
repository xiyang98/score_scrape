<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>The Double Concerto for Clarinet, Viola, and Orchestra in E minor, Op.88 was written for the composer's son Max Felix Bruch, clarinetist, and Willy Hess, violist. The complete, revised orchestral parts (rental only), the full score and a new piano reduction are available from Edition Peters (edited by Nicolai Pfeffer, 2008).
</p>
