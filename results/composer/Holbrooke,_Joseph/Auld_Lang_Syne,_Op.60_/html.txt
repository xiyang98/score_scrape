<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Other alternative titles: Scotch Variations, Portraits in Variations for Grand Orchestra, Portraits for Orchestra on a Scots Air. The 20 variations have titles using the initials of himself and 19 musical friends: 1.Joseph Holbrooke, 2. <a href="/wiki/Category:Bridge,_Frank" title="Category:Bridge, Frank">Frank Bridge</a>, 3. <a href="/wiki/Category:Bax,_Arnold" title="Category:Bax, Arnold">Arnold Bax</a>, 4.Samuel Coleridge-Taylor, 5.William Wallace/Vaughan Williams/Wilson Steer, 6. <a href="/wiki/Category:Dale,_Benjamin" title="Category:Dale, Benjamin">Benjamin Dale</a>, 7.John Saunders/Ethel Smyth, 8.Wilson Steer/Richard Walthew, 9.Edward Elgar, 10.Frederick Delius, 11.Joseph Speaight/Sidney H. Sime, 12.Sidney H. Sime/Frederick Charles Nichols/John Saunders, 13.Stanley Hawley, 14.Dan Godfrey/Havergal Brian, 15.Cecil Forsyth/Cyril Jenkins, 16.<a href="/wiki/Category:Scott,_Cyril" title="Category:Scott, Cyril">Cyril Scott</a>/Cyril Jenkins/CD?, 17. <a href="/wiki/Category:Strauss,_Richard" title="Category:Strauss, Richard">Richard Strauss</a>, 18.Augustus John/George Moore, 19.Joe Ivimey/John Herbert Foulds/George Moore, 20.<a href="/wiki/Category:Bantock,_Granville" title="Category:Bantock, Granville">Granville Bantock</a>. The alternatives are due to variations between versions and editions. Ref.: Joseph Holbrooke: Composer, Critic, and Musical Patriot, edited by Paul Watt and Anne-Marie Forbes, published by Rowman &amp; Littlefield, 2015.
</p>
