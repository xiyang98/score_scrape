<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p><i>Bronwen</i> is the third opera in Holbrooke's trilogy <i>The Cauldron of Annwn</i>.
</p><p>The opera was published in full score by J. &amp; W. Chester, 1929. The holograph full score of Act 1 is at the Grainger Museum, Melbourne, Australia (MG C2/H0L-10-1(Q)), the holograph full scores of Acts 2 and 3 are at the National Library of Wales (NLW MSS 23863-23865E).
</p>
