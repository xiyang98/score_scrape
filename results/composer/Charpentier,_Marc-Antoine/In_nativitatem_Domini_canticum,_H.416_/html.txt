<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>According to Grove Music, "Charpentiers dramatic motets number 35. They are more commonly known as oratorios  a misnomer, since they were composed as Latin motets for sacred services, not for meetings in oratories or for spiritual concerts."  Tagged as motet.
</p>
