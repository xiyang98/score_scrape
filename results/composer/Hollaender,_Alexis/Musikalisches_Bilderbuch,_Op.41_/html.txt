<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>

<ul><li>About 20 works with this title were published between 1850 and 1900. Judging by the number of reprints and arrangements, those by Hollaeneder, <a href="/wiki/Category:Dorn,_Alexander_Julius_Paul" title="Category:Dorn, Alexander Julius Paul">Alexander Julius Paul Dorn</a> (Opp.93, 116, 1880s) and <a href="/wiki/Musikalisches_Bilderbuch,_Op.11_(Volkmann,_Robert)" title="Musikalisches Bilderbuch, Op.11 (Volkmann, Robert)">Volkmann (Op.11, 1853)</a> were the most popular.
