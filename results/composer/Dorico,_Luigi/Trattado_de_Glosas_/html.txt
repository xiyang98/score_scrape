<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Treatise on ornamenting upon a cadence and breaking a ground, with examples of each. Also on composing ricercars on madrigals (motets, chansons), with examples.
</p>
