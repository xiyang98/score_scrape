<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<h4> <span class="mw-headline" id="About_the_Kram_Edition">About the Kram Edition</span></h4>
<p>Never realized what a wonderful melodic gift Graun had. Have made two versions available:
</p>
<ol><li> A performance version for orchestras that don't want to deal with all the ornaments (like schools for which a lot of this music is perfect)
</li><li> A more or less original transcription for the purist. No figures in the manuscript, but it works fine without them being in a pretty pre-classical style. 
</li></ol>
<p>I'll add a realization to the performance edition when I have time. Note only the Andante uses winds. The other two movements are for Strings and Continuo. 
</p>
