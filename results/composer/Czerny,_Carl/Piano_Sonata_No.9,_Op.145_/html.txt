<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Piano Sonatas 7-9 (E minor, E<span class="music-symbol" style="font-family: Arial Unicode MS, Lucida Sans Unicode; font-size:110%">&#x266d;</span> major, B minor) opp.143-145 were, one guesses, published in a group about the same time, ca.1827- date from the following review of Op.143 (by H[einrich?] Marschner) in the Berliner allgemeine musikalische zeitung, 19 December 1827, pp 410-11. (Op.143, at least- Op.144 too? - also a Fantasia en forme de Sonate. All are recorded now I think, so this can be checked?
</p><p>Discussed by Larson (see dissertation link) from p.97.
