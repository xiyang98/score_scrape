<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Actually his 3rd (at least) in "actual chronological" numbering, since a symphony in D major from about 1817 preceded his official C minor Op.780 "first". (The early D major may still be in manuscript, like some of the official "numbered" symphonies were until the last few years or so, and received possibly its first performance in over a century just a few years ago.)
</p>
