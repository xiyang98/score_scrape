<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Of several Bellini fantasies composed by Czerny (several of them on the opera I Puritani alone), Op.247 No.2 is the one played by Thomas Fischer on a 2008 Naxos Records CD (the Opus No. does not seem to be specified, at least not in the online description). - Schissel
</p>
