<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>First performance 1846, not 1849, according to <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://operone.de/komponist/hartmannjohpe.html">Operone</a>.
</p><p>Overture published by Lose in an arrangement in 1873.
</p><p>There is a vocal score of a 10-number (earlier, or later?) version of the opera (1 act) <span class="plainlinks"><a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="https://archive.org/details/lidenkirstenroma00hart">here</a></span>. (The Lose plate is also 2168.)
</p><p>Recorded in 1999 on the Dacapo CD label.
</p><p><span class="plainlinks"><a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://wayback-01.kb.dk/wayback/20101105080530/http://www2.kb.dk/elib/noder/hcamusik/liden_kirsten/index_en.htm">Initially</a></span> one act, then revised, expanded and divided into two acts in the late 1850s (by 1858 or so).
</p>
