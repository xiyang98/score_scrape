<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Composition of first and at least part of theme and variations composed before Stretta.
</p>
<dl><dd><i>-Paul Hindemith: A Research and Information Guide</i> 
</dd></dl>
<p>Autograph of the Stretta published as supplement to <i>Melos 1</i>, no.20 (December 1920). Remainder of autograph score lost; first movement and Theme and Variations reconstructed from sketches by Bernhard Billeter for the supplement volume to <i>SW</i> V, 9 and the performing edition. See notes for <i><a href="/wiki/Tanzst%C3%BCcke,_Op.19_(Hindemith,_Paul)" title="Tanzstücke, Op.19 (Hindemith, Paul)">Tanzstücke</a></i>, Op.19.
</p>
<dl><dd><i>-Paul Hindemith: A Research and Information Guide</i>
