<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<ul><li>Hindemith arranged six pieces for for easy piano in 1930 or 1931 (See Piano Suite above). According to Else Thalheimer-Lewertoff, Hindemith subsequently composed an interlude for the two composed an interlude for a two-act performance of the work (with new text by S. B. Lewertoff, resetting the work in Palestine) by the Jüdischer Kulturbund in Cologne in the mid-1930s.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Piano Suite: Arranged by Hindemith from the stage work.
</li></ul>
<dl><dd><i>-Paul Hindemith: A Research and Information Guide</i>
</dd></dl>
