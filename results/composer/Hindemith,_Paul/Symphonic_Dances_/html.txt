<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Apparently begun for a planned but unrealized ballet for Léonide Massine. In 1939 Hindemith incorporated the work in a draft scenario for a ballet to be called <i>Der Kinderkreuzzug</i> (The Children's Crusade), but this plan was also never realized.
</p>
<dl><dd>-<i>Paul Hindemith: A Research and Information Guide</i>
</dd></dl>
