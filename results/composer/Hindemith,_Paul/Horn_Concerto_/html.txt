<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>"The horn declaims a short poem in such a way that the note values correspond with the syllables of the verses..." (Dennis Brain: A Life in Music, p.169, quoting Norman del Mar). (Further from the authors: "It was not intended that the poem should be additionally recited by a speaker, though in...") (as in a recording for Nixa and a more recent CD recording for cpo.)
