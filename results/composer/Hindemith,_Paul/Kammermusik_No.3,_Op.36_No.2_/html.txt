<th>Misc. Notes
</th>
<td>For the Piano Part, I had a hard time lining up some pages with scanner to get even white borders around music. If the music looks like it is cut off on some pages on the right or left sides, all the music is there actually.<br />The Hindemith Foundation gives "late 1924" for the issue date of this piano reduction; is there really a ©1925 on it nevertheless? ("Erstausgabe des Klavierauszuges mit beigelegter Solo-Stimme: Ende August / Anfang September 1924". Which suggests that he was done with the "content" of the work by September 1924 and that instrumentation of it is what occupied him through early 1925.)
</td></tr>

<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Cello Concerto No.2, between Op.3 in E-flat and the Concerto of 1940 in G.
Although the piano reduction was published as the composer's work, Giselher Schubert notes in his introduction to the publication in <i>SW (Sämtliche Werke)</i> that it was actually prepared by his brother Rudolf.
</p>
