<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Premiere - as ballet? (Costumer mentioned, which seems irrelevant to a suite-only performance) (See Hindemith Foundation link.)
</p><p>Closer to a "play with music", as described @ MusicWeb &amp; Wikipedia, than an opera. "Weihnachtsmärchen" according to Hindemith. 
</p><p>The "Tanz der Holzpuppen" was published separately in a piano arrangement. A suite of instrumental excerpts was compiled by Franz Willms in 1925 and published as ED 5861 (BSS 42018) in 1969.
</p>
