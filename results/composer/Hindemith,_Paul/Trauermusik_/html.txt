<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Written in a few hours in the afternoon of 20th January 1936, and first performed the same evening, to commemorate the death of King George V of England. The first original full score was copyrighted 1936 but was published in 1937.
