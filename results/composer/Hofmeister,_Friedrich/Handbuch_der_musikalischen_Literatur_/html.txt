<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<h3> <span class="mw-headline" id="Publication_History">Publication History</span></h3>
<ul><li>First edition (1817). Carl Friedrich Whistling, editor.
</li></ul>
<dl><dd><ul><li>Supplements (Nachtrag) Nos.1–10 (1818–1827). Carl Friedrich Whistling, editor.
</li></ul>
</dd></dl>
<ul><li>Second (revised and enlarged) edition (1828). Carl Friedrich Whistling, editor.
</li></ul>
<dl><dd><ul><li>First supplement (Ergänzungsband) (1829). Carl Friedrich Whistling, editor.
</li><li>Second supplement (1834). Adolph Moritz Hofmeister and Fritz Whistling, editors.
</li><li>Third supplement (1839). Adolph Moritz Hofmeister and Fritz Whistling, editors.
</li></ul>
</dd></dl>
<ul><li>Third edition (1844–1845). Adolph Moritz Hofmeister, editor. 3 volumes (music for string and wind instruments; music for piano, organ, harp, and harmonica; vocal music).
</li></ul>
<dl><dd><ul><li>First supplement (1852, vol.4). Covering the years 1844–1851.
</li><li>Second supplement (1860, vol.5). Covering the years 1852–1859.
</li><li>Third supplement (1868, vol.6). Covering the years 1860–1867.
</li><li>Fourth supplement (1876, vol.7). Covering the years 1868–1873.
</li><li>Fifth supplement (1881, vol.8). Covering the years 1874–1879.
</li><li>Sixth supplement (1887, vol.9). Covering the years 1880–1885.
</li><li>Seventh supplement (1893, vol.10). Covering the years 1886–1891.
</li><li>Eighth supplement (1900, vol.11). Covering the years 1892–1897.
</li><li>Ninth supplement (1906, vol.12). Covering the years 1898–1903.
</li><li>Tenth supplement (1910, vol.13). Covering the years 1904–1908.
</li><li>Eleventh supplement (1915, vol.14). Covering the years 1909–1913.
</li><li>Twelfth supplement (1920–1921), vol.15). Covering the years 1914–1918.
</li><li>Thirteenth supplement (1924–1925), vol.16).
</li></ul>
</dd></dl>
<ul><li><i>Hofmeisters Handbuch der Musikliteratur</i>
</li></ul>
<dl><dd><ul><li>Vol.17 (1929).
</li><li>Vol.18 (1934–1935).
</li><li>Vol.19 (1943). Volume incomplete as the alphabetic listing only reaches "Lincke."
</li></ul>
</dd></dl>
