<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>This musical rendition of Aubign√©'s Sonnet <i>Extase</i>, with its biblical overtones in true <i>Death where is thy sting</i> manner, is taken from <i>L'h√©catombe √† Diane</i> (1573) the first part of his collection of Sonnets <i>Printemps</i> dedicated to Diane Salviati, a young Catholic, an impossible love on account of his being a converted Protestant.
