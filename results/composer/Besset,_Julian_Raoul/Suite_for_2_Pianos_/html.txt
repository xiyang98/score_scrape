<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>The first version was an initial approach to a 2 Piano composition. Since its inception, the work has gone through several tentative but unsatisfactory stages. The latest can be considered as more or less stable. Accordingly,  each movement is presented separately, instead of like the original one volume edition.
</p>
