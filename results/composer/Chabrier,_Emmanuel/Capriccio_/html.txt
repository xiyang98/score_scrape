<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Large-scale work begun by Chabrier in 1883 left unfinished at time of his death, and subsequently completed by Maurice Le Boucher in 1914. Publication also came in 1914 dedicated "à la mémoire de <a href="/wiki/Category:Pugno,_Raoul" title="Category:Pugno, Raoul">Raoul Pugno</a>". Unclear where Chabrier's work ended and Le Boucher's began.
</p>
