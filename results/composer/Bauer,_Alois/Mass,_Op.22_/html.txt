<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Mentioned in Fétis 1860 page 276 as one of 6 Messes de campagne (opp.22 &amp;25 - <i>possibly</i> based on this op.22 is a set rather than one mass.) (Actually while the Fétis books came out in 1860, the article on Bauer was written in 1854 ("actuellement (1854) vivant")- so the mass op.22 was written and given an opus number - and published?- by 1854.)
</p>
