<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Arranged from a cavatina withdrawn from the original revision of <a href="/wiki/Benvenuto_Cellini,_H_76_(Berlioz,_Hector)" title="Benvenuto Cellini, H 76 (Berlioz, Hector)"><i>Benvenuto Cellini</i></a> owing to the dislike of the <i>prima donna</i>.
</p>
<h4> <span class="mw-headline" id="Publication_History">Publication History</span></h4>
<ul><li>Paris: 1841, <a href="/wiki/Richault" title="Richault">Richault</a>, plate 6297.R., as Œuvre 8 (full score, orchestra parts, violin and piano arrangement)
</li><li>Leipzig: Breitkopf und Härtel, n.d. (1902), as part of Hector Berlioz Werke (1900–07), Serie III, Band VI, edited by Charles Malherbe and Felix Weingartner
</li><li>Kassel: Bärenreiter, 2005, BA 5461, as part of New Berlioz Edition (1967–2005), Volume 21, edited by Hugh Macdonald
</li><li>Vancouver: Edition Zeza, 2010, EZ-2153 (full score, XL score, set of parts)
</li></ul>
