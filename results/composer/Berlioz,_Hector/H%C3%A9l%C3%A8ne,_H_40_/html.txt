<ul><li>Paris: January 1830, as Œuvre 2, No.2 (vocal score)
</li><li>Paris: 1863, <b>H 40A</b> included in <i>Collection de 32 Mélodies</i>: no. 8
</li><li>Leipzig: <a href="/wiki/Breitkopf_und_H%C3%A4rtel" title="Breitkopf und Härtel">Breitkopf und Härtel</a>, n.d., as part of Hector Berlioz Werke (1900–07), Serie VI, Band 14 (1903) and Serie VII, Band 16 (1904), edited by Charles Malherbe and Felix Weingartner
</li><li>Kassel: <a href="/wiki/B%C3%A4renreiter" title="Bärenreiter">Bärenreiter</a>, as part of <a href="/wiki/Hector_Berlioz:_New_Berlioz_Edition" title="Hector Berlioz: New Berlioz Edition" class="mw-redirect">New Berlioz Edition</a> (1967–2005)
<dl><dd><b>H 40B</b> in BA 5452/I, Volume 12a (1991), edited by Julian Rushton
</dd><dd><b>H 40A</b> in BA 5455, Volume 15 (2005), edited by Ian Rumbold
</dd></dl>
</li></ul>
