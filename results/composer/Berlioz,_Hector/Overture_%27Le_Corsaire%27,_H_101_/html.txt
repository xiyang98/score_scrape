<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Composed 1844, and originally entitled <i>La Tour de Nice</i> (The Tower of Nice), then <i>Le Corsaire Rouge</i> (The Red Corsair), first performance of initial version (H 101A), Paris, Cirque Olympique, 19 January 1845.<br />Revised before 1852, first performance of revised version (H 101B), Brunswick, 8 April 1854.
</p>
