<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<ul><li>"Istrumente da penna" are "quilled" instruments, e.g. harpsichord.  The keyboard tag will cover all of these instruments.
</li><li>In the original collection, this (along with some other works) are in 4-part open score, but according to Barbara Sachs, in her entry on Diruta in Igor Kipnis' <i>The Harpsichord and Clavichord:  An Encyclopedia</i>, these can be played on harpsichord.
</li></ul>
