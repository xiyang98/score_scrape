<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>This music is for an early childhood music classroom.  It is music for a music educator dedicated to providing music instruction for the very young.  The music is catagorized as 1) Songs with Words, 2) Songs without Words, 3) Chants with Words, 4) Chants without Words.
</p>
