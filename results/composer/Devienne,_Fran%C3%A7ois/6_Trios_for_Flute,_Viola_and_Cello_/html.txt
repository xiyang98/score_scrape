<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>These are not apparently meant to be played as independent works&#160;; the end of each trio contains the instruction that the first movement of the next should follow without a pause ("volti trio ...").
</p>
