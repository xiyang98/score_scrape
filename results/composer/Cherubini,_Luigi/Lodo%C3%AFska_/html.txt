<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Worldcat lists at least 3 complete(ish?) recordings of the opera- on Sony (1991), on Nuova Era (1965 performance, distributed 1989), and one from Polskie Radio (2008) (with limited distribution, perhaps). Timings of the first two, at least, seem to be about the same.
</p>
