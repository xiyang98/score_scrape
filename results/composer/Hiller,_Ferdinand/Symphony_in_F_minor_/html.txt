<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>"Opus 4" is just the general name of the website on which this is found; the symphonies in C and in E minor also downloadable from the same website also have "Opus 4" on the same page. There is no opus 4 on the scanned score. (Actually, this seems to have been finished sometime between the first piano quartet (op.3, 1832) and the first string quartet (op.13), in 1833.)
</p><p>Re 1832 October see <span class="plainlinks"><a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://publikationen.ub.uni-frankfurt.de/frontdoor/index/index/docId/22747">Composer's Thematic Catalog 1832-1837</a></span>, page 5 (entered in his catalog on 22 October 1832/July 1833).
