<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Notes to the Cavi recording do not give a date more specific than 1873 (publication date) for the piano quintet. No premiere date is given.
</p><p><span class="plainlinks"><a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://books.google.com/books?id=2DdWAAAAcAAJ&amp;pg=PA23">Leipziger Zeitung 1873</a></span> (article: Musikalisches aus Leipzig aus dem Jahre 1872) mentions a performance (and mentions <a href="/wiki/Category:R%C3%B6ntgen,_Julius" title="Category:RÃ¶ntgen, Julius">Julius RÃ¶ntgen</a>'s name in association with it?) Also, performed 12 November 1872 in KÃ¶ln (see <span class="plainlinks"><a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://books.google.com/books?id=QkEfAAAAMAAJ&amp;pg=PA718">Musikalisches Wochenblatt, Volume 3, Part 2</a></span>, 13 December 1872, p.810, Berichte. Quintet mentioned as being in manuscript in Nov. 1872, so 1873 is indeed the <i>first</i> publication date.)
</p>
