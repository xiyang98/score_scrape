<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>The piece is to be heard in E<span class="music-symbol" style="font-family: Arial Unicode MS, Lucida Sans Unicode; font-size:110%">&#x266d;</span> major, but the scoring for viol (and cello) is in D major.  The violin therefore needs to be tuned half a tone lower than the other instruments. (See notes in score.) This is probably to soften the sound of the violin, allowing for the viol to have a more equal sound within the ensemble.
</p>
