<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>According to Walburga Litschauer as translated by Eugene Hartzell, "Mendelssohn's "Venetianisches Gondollied" op.30/6 was evidently the model. The two compositions correspond to a great degree in form, key and metre, and in their lyrical mood."
