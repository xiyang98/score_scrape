<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<ul><li> In the first edition of the revised version of 1888, the ordering for the 4 compositions deviates from the original ordering by the composer. The WAB ordering for the 4 compositions, as it is on the score, deviates also from the original ordering by the composer, as currently published by the <i>Bruckner Gesamtausgabe</i>. See <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://www.brucknerdiskografie.nl/php/index.php?pag=167">Discography by Hans Roelofs</a>. The fifth Tantum ergo in the fascicule is the 1888 version of <a href="/wiki/Tantum_ergo,_WAB_42_(Bruckner,_Anton)" title="Tantum ergo, WAB 42 (Bruckner, Anton)">Tantum ergo, WAB 42</a>. 
</li></ul>
