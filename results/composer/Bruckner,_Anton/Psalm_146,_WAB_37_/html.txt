<ul><li>The <i>Reinschrift</i> Mus.Hs.6011 was made by the copist of St. Florian, who also made <i>Reinschrifte</i> of Bruckner's <a href="/wiki/Psalm_114,_WAB_36_(Bruckner,_Anton)" title="Psalm 114, WAB 36 (Bruckner, Anton)">Psalm 114</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Magnificat,_WAB_24_(Bruckner,_Anton)" title="Magnificat, WAB 24 (Bruckner, Anton)">Magnificat</a>.(C. van Zwol, p. 697) 
</li><li>The <i>Reinschrift</i> Mus.Hs.19701 is presumably the score, which Riedelbauch used for his historical performance of 1971. As indicated on the score, Riedelbauch used for his studio recording of 1972 two separate SATB choirs in part III (<i>GroÃŸ ist unser Herr</i>), which provided a nice canon effect.
</li></ul>
