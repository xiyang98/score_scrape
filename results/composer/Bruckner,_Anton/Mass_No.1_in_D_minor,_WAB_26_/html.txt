<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<ul><li> Four weeks after the first performance, the Mass was performed again during a "Concert spirituel" in the <i>Linzer Redoutensaal</i>. Because there was no organ available in the <i>Redoutensaal</i>, Bruckner composed an alternative with woodwinds (clarinets and bassoons) for the short organ intermezzo in the mid-section of the Credo (see: <span class="plainlinks"><a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://data.onb.ac.at/rec/AL00227370">Österreichische Nationalbibliothek, Mus.Hs.3170</a></span>).
</li><li> <b>Caveat:</b> In the copy of Johann Gross' edition (#113875) and its segment 3 (#89618), pages 111 &amp; 112 of the score are put between pages 108 and 109. --<a href="/wiki/User:Meneerke_bloem" title="User:Meneerke bloem">Réginald alias Meneerke bloem</a> <small>(<a href="/index.php?title=User_talk:Meneerke_bloem&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="User talk:Meneerke bloem (page does not exist)">To reply</a>)</small> 17:05, 16 September 2016 (CEST)
