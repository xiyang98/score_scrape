<p><br />
<b>Text:</b>
</p><p><i>Christus factus est pro nobis obediens,</i><br />
<i>usque ad mortem, mortem autem crucis.</i><br />
<i>Propter quod et Deus exaltavit illum et dedit illi nomen,</i><br />
<i>quod est super omne nomen.</i>
</p><p><br />
Christ became obedient for us unto death,<br />
even to the death, death on the cross.<br />
Therefore God exalted Him and gave Him a name<br />
which is above all names.
</p>
