<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<ul><li> Berberich's score is using the older text of the hymn. Reuter's score is using the modernised text of the hymn, as it is issued in the <i>Gesamtausgebe</i> Band XXI/29, but is missing the 4-bar <i>Amen</i>, which was added at the end of the motet. --<a href="/wiki/User:Meneerke_bloem" title="User:Meneerke bloem">Réginald alias Meneerke bloem</a> <small>(<a href="/index.php?title=User_talk:Meneerke_bloem&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="User talk:Meneerke bloem (page does not exist)">To reply</a>)</small> 16:37, 7 July 2016 (EDT)
</li></ul>
