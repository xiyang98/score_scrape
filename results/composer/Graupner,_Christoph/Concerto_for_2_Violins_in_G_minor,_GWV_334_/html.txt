<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>A Graupner double concerto for 2 violins in this key has been published in a 1996 A-R editions volume (given No.47 of Graupner's concertos?) - <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://obis.oberlin.edu/record=b1852177~S4">here</a> is a link to a library catalog description of the volume and <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="https://www.areditions.com/rr/rrb/b078.html">here</a> is the volume at A-R Editions). (Though that could also be GWV 335. RISM and Wikipedia don't list any other 2 violin concertos in this key by Graupner, but there might be others still.) WitG = 28 in Martin Witte's 1963 <i>Die Instrumentalkonzerte von Johann Christoph Graupner (1683-1760).</i> (according again to RISM)&#160;; applies only to Graupner's instrumental concertos, of course. (I see they list "No.28" in the A-R volume, which would be WitG 28, which is this one. So it -is- in that volume, in score, with parts available at request from A-R. Hrm. Hopefully has been published before that, though, and almost certainly performed, though that is not quite the same.)
</p>
