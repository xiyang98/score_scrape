<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>While the 2nd movement seems to be original to this trio, the other movements are arrangements from his piano sonatas.<br />Movement 1 is the first movement from from <i><a href="/wiki/Divertimento_in_G_major,_Hob.XVI:8_(Haydn,_Joseph)" title="Divertimento in G major, Hob.XVI:8 (Haydn, Joseph)">Divertimento in G major, Hob.XVI:8</a></i> transposed to F major.<br />Movement 3 is the first movement from from <i><a href="/wiki/Divertimento_in_F_major,_Hob.XVI:9_(Haydn,_Joseph)" title="Divertimento in F major, Hob.XVI:9 (Haydn, Joseph)">Divertimento in F major, Hob.XVI:9</a></i>.<br />Movement 4 Menuetto is the second movement from from <i><a href="/wiki/Divertimento_in_F_major,_Hob.XVI:9_(Haydn,_Joseph)" title="Divertimento in F major, Hob.XVI:9 (Haydn, Joseph)">Divertimento in F major, Hob.XVI:9</a></i>.<br />Movement 4 Trio is the Minuet (second movement) from from <i><a href="/wiki/Divertimento_in_A_major,_Hob.XVI:5_(Haydn,_Joseph)" title="Divertimento in A major, Hob.XVI:5 (Haydn, Joseph)">Divertimento in A major, Hob.XVI:5</a></i> transposed from A major to A minor.<br />Movement 5 is the third movement from from <i><a href="/wiki/Divertimento_in_F_major,_Hob.XVI:9_(Haydn,_Joseph)" title="Divertimento in F major, Hob.XVI:9 (Haydn, Joseph)">Divertimento in F major, Hob.XVI:9</a></i>.
