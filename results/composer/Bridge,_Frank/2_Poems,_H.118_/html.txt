<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>These orchestral poems were inspired by <a href="/index.php?title=Category:Jefferies,_John_Richard&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Category:Jefferies, John Richard (page does not exist)">John Richard Jefferies</a> (1848-1887), and quotes from <i>The Open Air</i> and <i>The Story of My Heart</i> are printed before each orchestral poem respectively.
</p>
