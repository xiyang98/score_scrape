<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<h4> <span class="mw-headline" id="Original_Text">Original Text</span></h4>
<dl><dd>Strike, seraphs, strike your harps of gold,
</dd><dd>And bow before th'eternal throne,
</dd><dd>Let heav'n's mysterious love be told,
</dd><dd>And make redemption's glories known.
</dd></dl>
<p><br />
</p>
<dl><dd>The shepherd swains on Judah's plains
</dd><dd>Have seen, from heav'n descending,
</dd><dd>An angel band, at God's command,
</dd><dd>Their harps and voices blending.
</dd></dl>
<p><br />
</p>
<dl><dd>Then haste to Bethl'em and behold
</dd><dd>The Saviour in his low estate.
</dd><dd>Yet bring Arabia's gifts of gold
</dd><dd>To deck his bed with regal state,
</dd><dd>And worship at his infant shrine
</dd><dd>Whose name and honours are divine.
</dd></dl>
