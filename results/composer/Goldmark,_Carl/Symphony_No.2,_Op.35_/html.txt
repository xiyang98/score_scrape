<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Dates 1887 and 1889 from Marco Polo program notes (uncredited; online) to their 1986 recording of this work.  The symphony has been recorded at least twice, once (1986) conducted by Halasz on Marco Polo, once by Yondani Butt on ASV (in 1995). Orchestration from <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://know.freelibrary.org">Free Library of Philadelphia</a> catalog. - Schissel
</p>
