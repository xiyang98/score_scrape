<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<ul><li>Józefina Weinert was daughter of <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/pl:Antoni_Weinert" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:pl:Antoni Weinert">Antoni Weinert</a> (1751—1850), Polish flutist and composer of Czech origin.
</li><li> Some works from this collection were recorded by Urszula Bartkiewicz for DUX: <span class="plainlinks"><a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://www.dux.pl/-polska-muzyka-klawesynowa-vol-i.html">CD 1 (Elsner)</a></span>, <span class="plainlinks"><a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://www.dux.pl/-polska-muzyka-klawesynowa-vol-ii.html">CD 2 (minor composers)</a></span>.
</li><li>The tags are incomplete, because not all volumes have been uploaded, so the "undetermined instrumentation" tag is for the missing items.
</li></ul>
