<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>The other piece of music called "Quadrilles" contains the same tunes as this one. "Quadrilles" was published well after Foster's death and is arranged for piano. "Old Folks Quadrilles" was arranged by Foster for The Social Orchestra.
</p>
