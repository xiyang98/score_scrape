<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>This is a re-typeset of the 1853 "Old Folks Quadrilles". These same quadrilles under the same title were used in the 1854 work "The Social Orchestra", but arranged for Flute, Violin and accompaniment.
</p>
