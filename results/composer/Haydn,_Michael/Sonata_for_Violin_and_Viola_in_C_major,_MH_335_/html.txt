<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Part of a set of six Duos for Archbishop Colloredo, of which Haydn wrote four and W. A. Mozart wrote the remaining two (K. 423 and K. 424). In 1813, Sieber published Michael's four Duos but attributed them to his brother Joseph.<br />M Haydn's complete set is <a href="/wiki/4_Sonatas_for_Violin_and_Viola_(Haydn,_Michael)" title="4 Sonatas for Violin and Viola (Haydn, Michael)">here</a>
</p><p>Artaria's apparently? earlier publication <span class="plainlinks"><a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://www.worldcat.org/oclc/638658135">here</a></span>.
</p>
