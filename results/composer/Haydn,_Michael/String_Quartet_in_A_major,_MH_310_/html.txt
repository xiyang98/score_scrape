<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>One of a set of six. Some have doubted these Quartets were really written by Michael Haydn, such as Paul Griffiths in his book <i>The String Quartet</i>.
</p>
<ul><li>Published by <a href="/wiki/Doblinger" title="Doblinger">Doblinger</a> in 1971 in an edition by Helmut Zehetmair.
</li></ul>
