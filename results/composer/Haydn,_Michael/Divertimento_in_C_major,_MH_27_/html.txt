<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Allmusic estimates 1764–ca.1770 for composition and describes it as a "Divertimento for violin, cello &amp; continuo". 1758–1760 estimate given by haydn.dk which describes this as an orchestral, not a chamber, work.
</p>
