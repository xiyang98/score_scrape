<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<ul><li>The fourth part may very well already be effectively a bass line imitating the continuo part, so the continuo probably only needs keyboard, not keyboard + another bass instrument.
</li><li>Grove Music does not list a continuo part, just "4 vc/viol/bn (c1734)".
</li><li>The parts are originally titled as follows: violoncello o fagotto p.mo, violoncello IIo , violoncello terzo , organo o violoncello 4o. (BnF catalogue général - Notice bibliographique)
</li></ul>
