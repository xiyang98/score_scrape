<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>First movement incomplete
</p><p>Possibly first published in the Peters Organ works series? Peter Williams (<i>The Organ Music of J. S. Bach</i>, from p.196) gives for sources, as the autograph does not survive-
</p><p>"complete in P 287 (J P Kellner, after 1727?); also, via CPE Bach (P290, P277?, Am.B59?), and [a] lost MS used in Peters I; first movement only [survives] in copies <i>via</i> Kittel(?)" (note: the Darmstadt digitized collection, of works by JS and CPE Bach, is possibly one of the above- P290, P277 or Am.B59?)
</p><p>(Not the only work in regards to which Williams mentions the possibility that Griepenkerl may have used a source in preparing his edition that is now lost. This is also possible in regards several of Rust's editions for the BGA but with Rust as editor other issues arise.)
</p>
