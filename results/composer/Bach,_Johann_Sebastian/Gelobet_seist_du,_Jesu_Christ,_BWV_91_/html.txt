<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<h4> <span class="mw-headline" id="Commentary_on_the_movements">Commentary on the movements</span></h4>
<ol><li>[Chorale]: Gelobet seist du, Jesu Christ. A festive opening chorus, although rather short
</li><li>Recitativo (Soprano): Der Glanz der höchsten Herrlichkeit. Secco recitative with bits of chorale interspersed
</li><li>Aria (Tenor): Gott, dem der Erden Kreis zu klein. Three baroque oboes (tuning!) are the only other instruments aside from continuo here
</li><li>Recitativo (Bass): O Christenheit! Recitative with string accompaniment
</li><li>Aria (Soprano, Alto): Die Armut, so Gott auf sich nimmt. The marked contrasts of the text are here illustarted
</li><li>Choral: Das hat er alles uns getan. A plain chorale setting with independent horn parts
</li></ol>
