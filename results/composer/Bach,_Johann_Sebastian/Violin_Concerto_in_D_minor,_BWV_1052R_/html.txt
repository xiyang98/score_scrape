<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<ul><li> The original score of the concerto has been lost, but it was subsequently arranged by Bach as the <i><a href="/wiki/Harpsichord_Concerto_No.1_in_D_minor,_BWV_1052_(Bach,_Johann_Sebastian)" title="Harpsichord Concerto No.1 in D minor, BWV 1052 (Bach, Johann Sebastian)">Harpsichord Concerto No.1 in D minor</a></i>, BWV 1052. On the basis of the latter, it has been reconstructed by <a href="/wiki/Category:David,_Ferdinand" title="Category:David, Ferdinand">Ferdinand David</a> (1873), <a href="/wiki/Category:Busoni,_Ferruccio" title="Category:Busoni, Ferruccio">Ferruccio Busoni</a> (1899) and <a href="/index.php?title=Category:Reitz,_Robert&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Category:Reitz, Robert (page does not exist)">Robert Reitz</a> (1917), among others.
