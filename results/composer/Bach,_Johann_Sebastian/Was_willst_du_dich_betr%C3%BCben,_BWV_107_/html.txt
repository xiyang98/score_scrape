<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<h4> <span class="mw-headline" id="Commentary_on_the_movements">Commentary on the movements</span></h4>
<ol><li>Chorus: Was willst du dich betrüben. A standard chorale cantata opening chorus—the sopranos sing the melody plain with a lively fugal texture below. 
</li><li>Recitativo (Bass): Denn Gott verlässet keinen. Recitative (the only one!) with 2 oboes d'amore. 
</li><li>Aria (Bass): Auf ihn magst du es wagen. An aria in <i>Bar</i> form with string accompaniment. 
</li><li>Aria (Tenor): Wenn auch gleich aus der Höllen. A continuo aria in a lively 3/4. 
</li><li>Aria (Soprano): Es richt's zu seinen Ehren. Two obbligato oboes d'amore provide a beautiful accompaniment to this aria. 
</li><li>Aria (Tenor): Darum ich mich ihm ergebe. The tenor declaims the chorale plain. 
</li><li>Choral: Herr, gib, daß ich dein Ehre. The chorus sings the chorale plain while the orchestral plays "florid siciliano rhythm" against it<sup>1</sup>. 
</li></ol>
