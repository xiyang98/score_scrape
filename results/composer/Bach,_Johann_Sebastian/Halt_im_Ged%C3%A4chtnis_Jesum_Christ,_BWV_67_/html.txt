<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<h4> <span class="mw-headline" id="Commentary_on_the_movements">Commentary on the movements</span></h4>
<ol><li>Chorus: Halt im Gedächtnis Jesum Christ. A densely woven, structurally symmetrical movement full of word-painting and imagery. 
</li><li>Aria (Tenor): Mein Jesus ist erstanden. The theme of this movement is full of sharply articulated repeated notes, which provide momentum. 
</li><li>Recitativo (Alto): Mein Jesu, heißest du des Todes Gift. Secco recitative. 
</li><li>Choral: Erschienen ist der herrlich Tag. A plain chorale setting. 
</li><li>Recitativo (Alto): Doch scheinet fast. Secco recitative. 
</li><li>Aria[/Chorus] (Bass): Friede sei mit euch! A chorus with the same music for each of the three strophes; each is prefixed by the bass solemnly intoning "Peace be with you!" 
</li><li>Choral: Du Friedefürst, Herr Jesu Christ. A plain choral setting. 
</li></ol>
