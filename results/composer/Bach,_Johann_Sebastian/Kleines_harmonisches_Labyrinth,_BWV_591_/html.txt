<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Bach's authorship is doubted by Philipp Spitta and Günter Haußwald; they suggested the piece was possibly composed by <a href="/wiki/Category:Heinichen,_Johann_David" title="Category:Heinichen, Johann David">Johann David Heinichen</a>, but without any substantial argument or musicological proof. (See W. Schmieder, <i>BWV</i> (1990), p. 546 &amp; 547). However, from a stylistic point of view, the piece sounds much more classical than baroque and could have been composed during the late 18th century or the beginning of the 19th century.
