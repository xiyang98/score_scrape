<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>For the 21st Sunday after Trinity
</p>
<h4> <span class="mw-headline" id="Commentary_on_the_movements">Commentary on the movements</span></h4>
<ol><li>Chorale: Was Gott tut, das ist Wohlgethan. A plain chorale setting, around which instrumental parts play separate material. Also, the first violin is the only "real" string part. 
</li><li>Recitativo (Tenor): Ach Gott! ween wirst du mich einmal. Secco recitative. 
</li><li>Aria (Soprano): Hört, ihr Augen, auf zu weinen. Obbligato oboe underlines the mournful air of the movement. However, the 3/8 time gives a lilt that would otherwise be sorely lacking. 
</li><li>Recitativo (Alto): Gott hat ein Herz, das des Erbarmens überfluß. Secco recitative. 
</li><li>Aria (Bass): Meinen Jesum laß ich nicht. Unison violins are the obbligato to this energetic aria. 
</li></ol>
