<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<h4> <span class="mw-headline" id="Commentary_on_the_movements">Commentary on the movements</span></h4>
<ol><li>Chorus: Ich glaube, lieber Herr, hilf meinem Unglauben. A movement "strikingly loose in construction" notable for virtuoso figuration everywhere. 
</li><li>Recitativo (Tenor): Des Herren Hand ist ja noch nicht verkürzt. Secco recitative. 
</li><li>Aria (Tenor): Wie zweifelhaftig ist mein Hoffen. First violin comes out of the string texture more here. 
</li><li>Recitativo (Alto): O fasse dich, du zweifelhafter Mut. Secco recitative.
</li><li>Aria (Alto): Der Heiland kennet ja die Seinen. A saraband-like dance aria, becoming progressively more and more animated.
</li><li>Choral: Wer hofft in Gott und dem vertraut. The soprano sings the tune unadorned, while the other voices ornament their parts heavily.
</li></ol>
