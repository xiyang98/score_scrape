<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<ul><li> Bach's authorship of this work is uncertain.  Some scholars feel it was by <a href="/wiki/Category:Bach,_Wilhelm_Friedemann" title="Category:Bach, Wilhelm Friedemann">Wilhelm Friedemann Bach</a>, and it does appear on some W. F. Bach recordings. Others are only prepared to say it is in the "school" of J.S. Bach (David Schulenberg, Wagner College, NY) <a rel="nofollow" class="external autonumber" href="http://www.americanbachsociety.org/meetings/madison_abstracts.html">[1]</a>. while the 1990 edition of the <i>Bach Werke Verzeichnis</i> (BWV) stated that there was insufficient evidence to place this in the "doubtful" or "spurious" categories of J. S. Bach's works.
</li></ul>
