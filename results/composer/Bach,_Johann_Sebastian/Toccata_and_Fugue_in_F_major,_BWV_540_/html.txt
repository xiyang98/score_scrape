<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Published in Band III of the Griepenkerl/Roitzsch Orgelwerke Edition released in 1844/1845 (first 3 volumes- more volumes released ca.1900) by C.F. Peters. (However, as the review of this in NZM points out, this work appeared under Peters' imprint earlier still, in 1832 to be specific:  see <span class="plainlinks"><a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://anno.onb.ac.at/cgi-content/anno-buch?apm=0&amp;aid=1000001&amp;bd=0001832&amp;teil=0203&amp;seite=00000042&amp;zoom=1">Hofmeister's <i>Monatsbericht</i> (1832), p.42</a></span> - toccatas and fugues for organ or piano in F and C, fantasy in D (minor?) for organ or piano, etc.)
