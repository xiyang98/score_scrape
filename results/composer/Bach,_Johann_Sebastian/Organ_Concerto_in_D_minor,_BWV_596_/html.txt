<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Previously attributed to W. F. Bach. However, the handwriting is undoubtedly JSB's, who seems to have been the copyist and most probably the arranger. See Alfred Einstein's introduction to the <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://petrucci.mus.auth.gr/imglnks/usimg/c/c2/IMSLP127910-PMLP126416-Vivaldi_-_Concerto_RV565_FSEulen_rsl.pdf">Eulenburg edition</a> of the Vivaldi <i>Concerto Grosso</i> RV 565 for a discussion.
