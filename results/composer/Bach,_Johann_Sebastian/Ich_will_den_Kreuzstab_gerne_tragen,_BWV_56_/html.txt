<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<h4> <span class="mw-headline" id="Commentary_on_the_movements">Commentary on the movements</span></h4>
<ol><li>Aria: Ich will den Kreuzstab gerne tragen. An aria in <i>Bar</i> form. The expressivity of this thematic material also fits the text beautifully. The <i>Abgesang</i> section is truly different from the ‘’Stollen’’ here. 
</li><li>Recitativo: Mein Wandel auf der Welt. A recitative with obbligato cello illustrating the motion of the water. This accompaniment stops at “So tret ich aus dem Schiff in meine Stadt”<sup>1</sup>. 
</li><li>Aria: Endlich, endlich wird mein Joch. In this, the longest movement of the cantata, Bach uses the pastoral and relaxed colour of solo oboe to great effect, forming a beautiful contrast to the agitated movements preceding. 
</li><li>Recitativo: Ich stehe fertig und bereit. A reprise of part of the first movement, with a recitative introduction. 
</li><li>Choral: Komm, o Tod, du Schlafes Bruder. A plain chorale setting. However, this is “an exceptional masterpiece…truly inspired…We seem able to perceive directly the soul passing through the pain of death into glory.”<sup>1</sup>
</li></ol>
