<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>The occasion, if any, for which the march was composed (which might provide a clue as to the composition date) is not stated (German unification was not completed until early 1871, and the Franco-Prussian War began in 1870 but likewise was not concluded until 1871- perhaps hope of Prussian victory?) - Schissel
</p>
