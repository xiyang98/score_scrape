<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Contains 42 musical numbers of which only five were newly composed works by Arne. The other music is made up of 13 pieces borrowed from Arne's earlier stage works, a new overture was by C. F. Abel, and 23 songs by other composers, including Boyce, Geminiani, Giordani, and Galuppi, albeit with new texts.
</p>
