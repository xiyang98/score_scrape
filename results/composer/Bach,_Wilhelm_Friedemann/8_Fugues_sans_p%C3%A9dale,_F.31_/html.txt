<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Instrumentation - The copy (abschrift) of F31/3 at Staatsbibliothek Berlin, for instance, seems to be for fortepiano (see <span class="plainlinks"><a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="https://opac.rism.info/metaopac/search?q=rism452505880&amp;db=251&amp;View=rism&amp;Language=en">RISM 452505880</a></span>)&#160;; that of the whole set in Kiev is for organ; another copy of no.1 also in Kiev is for harpsichord, likewise a copy of no.6 in Brussels. So it does not seem clear that the whole set is for organ and only that!
</p>
