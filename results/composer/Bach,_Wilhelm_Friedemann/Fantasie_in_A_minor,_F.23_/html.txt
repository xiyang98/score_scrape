<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Ms copy at Schsische Landesbibliothek in Dresden (SLUB) (and a copy of that at Harvard's Loeb Library), along with F. 14, 20, 21.
</p><p>First?? published, as "Divertimento" (in A minor, 1763), in 1891, by Cohen of Bonn (<span class="plainlinks"><a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://anno.onb.ac.at/cgi-content/anno-buch?apm=0&amp;aid=1000001&amp;bd=0001891&amp;teil=0203&amp;seite=00000463&amp;zoom=1">Hofmeister's <i>Monatsbericht</i> (1891), p.463</a></span>; Worldcat appearance shows 1763 is in title) (possibly earlier publication too), though better-known in a Schott  edition of 1972 in which the title Fantasia is used. (Fairly sure these are the same works; not positive what title was Bach's originally.)
</p>
