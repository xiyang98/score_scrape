<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<ul><li>Doubtful authenticity, but included in Chrysander's edition
</li><li>First pub 1870s according to BSB catalog, but other Handel trio sonata arrs date from 1890s
</li><li>Grove Music, though listing this in the "Doubtful and spurious" category, says the original instrumentation was oboe, violin, and continuo. Therefore tagged for both possibilities.
</li></ul>
