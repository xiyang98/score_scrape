<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Grove Music lists this as a fragment of a sonata, and the instrumentation as "[vn]".  It also says the movement is "related to 4th movt of <a href="/wiki/Recorder_Sonata_in_A_minor,_HWV_362_(Handel,_George_Frideric)" title="Recorder Sonata in A minor, HWV 362 (Handel, George Frideric)">hwv362</a> and 4th movt of <a href="/wiki/Trio_Sonata_in_G_minor,_HWV_387_(Handel,_George_Frideric)" title="Trio Sonata in G minor, HWV 387 (Handel, George Frideric)">Trio sonata hwv387</a>".
