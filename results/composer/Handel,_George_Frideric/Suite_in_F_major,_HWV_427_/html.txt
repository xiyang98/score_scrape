<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>According to Grove Music, all of Handel's keyboard works were probably written for harpsichord, and the <i>Suites de pièces pour le clavecin</i> published in London in 1720 consisted of partly new, partly earlier material.
</p>
