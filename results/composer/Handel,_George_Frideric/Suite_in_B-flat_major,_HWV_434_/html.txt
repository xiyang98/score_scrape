<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<ul><li>According to Grove Music, all of Handel's keyboard works were probably written for harpsichord, and the <i>Suites de Pièces pour le Clavecin</i> published in London in 1720 consisted of partly new, partly earlier material. 
</li><li>The <i>Minuet</i> in G minor found after the <i>Aria con Variazioni</i> does not belong to this suite.
</li></ul>
