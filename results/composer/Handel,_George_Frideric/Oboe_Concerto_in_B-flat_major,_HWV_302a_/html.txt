<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<ul><li>The 1st and 2nd movements were taken from the instrumental introduction to Chandos Anthem No.8, HWV 253. The last two movements are identical to the overture to the Chandos Anthem No.5, HWV 250c.  Published in the collection "Select Harmony" by John Walsh, possibly without Handel's approval.
</li></ul>
