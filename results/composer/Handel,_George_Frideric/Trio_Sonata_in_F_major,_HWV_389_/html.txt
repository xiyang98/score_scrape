<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<ul><li>Though a Wikipedia article lists this trio sonata as of doubtful authenticity (i.e. not by Handel), it isn't listed that way in the (more authoritative) Grove Music.  See <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_compositions_by_George_Frideric_Handel#Trio_sonatas" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:List of compositions by George Frideric Handel">Wikipedia</a>
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Grove also lists this work with the alternative instruments for the top part.
</li></ul>
