<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<ul><li> Contemporaneously referred to as either <i>The Orchestra Concerto</i> or <i>The Second Overture in Amadigi</i>. The former name was given, because it was performed "For the Benefit of the Instrumental Musick" at the King's Theatre benefit night.
</li><li> In early copies of 1st edition of <a href="/wiki/6_Concerti_Grossi,_Op.3_(Handel,_George_Frideric)" title="6 Concerti Grossi, Op.3 (Handel, George Frideric)">Op.3</a>, a spurious concerto (printed as "Op.3 No.4a") was accidentally substituted. This spurious concerto (composer is anonymous) is now referred to as "Op.3 No.4b". Walsh included it instead in his 3rd collection of <i>Select Harmony</i>.
</li><li> The first <i>Minuetto</i> (IV.) exists also in 3 slightly different adaptations for keyboard solo as <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="https://imslp.org/wiki/Minuet_in_F_major,_HWV_516_(Handel,_George_Frideric)">HWV 516a, b, c</a>.
</li></ul>
