<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<ul><li>According to Grove, the version for harp was the original - performed with <i>Alexander’s Feast</i> and later published as an organ concerto. 
</li><li>The harp version features recorders or flutes, while the organ version often uses oboes instead.
</li></ul>
