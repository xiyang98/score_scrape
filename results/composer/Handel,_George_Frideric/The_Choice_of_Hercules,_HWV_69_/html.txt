<th><span class="mh555">Movements/Sections</span><span class="ms555">Mov'ts/Sec's</span>
</th>
<td>2 parts
<dl><dt><ul><li>Part 1
</li></ul>
</dt><dd>1. Overture
</dd><dd>2. Accompagnato: See, Hercules! how smiles yon myrtle plain (Pleasure)
</dd><dd>3. Aria: Come, blooming boy, with me repair (Pleasure)
</dd><dd>4. Aria: There the brisk sparkling nectar repair (Pleasure)
</dd><dd>5. Solo and Chorus
<dl><dd>a. Solo: While for thy arms that Beauty glows (Pleasure)
</dd><dd>b. Chorus: Seize, seize these blessings, blooming boy! (Chorus)
</dd></dl>
</dd><dd>Recitative: Away, mistaken wretch, away! (Virtue)
</dd><dd>6. Aria: This manly youth's exalted mind (Virtue)
</dd><dd>Recitative: Rise youth! exalt thyself and me (Virtue)
</dd><dd>7. Aria: Go, assert thy heav'nly race (Virtue)
</dd><dd>Recitative: In peace, in war, pursue thy country's good (Virtue)
</dd><dd>8. Solo and Chorus
<dl><dd>a. Solo: So shalt thou gain immortal praise (Virtue)
</dd><dd>b. Chorus: The golden trump of Fame
</dd></dl>
</dd><dd>Recitative: Hearst thou, what dangers then thou must engage? (Pleasure)
</dd><dd>9. Solo and Chorus
<dl><dd>a. Solo: Turn thee, youth, to joy and love (Pleasure)
</dd><dd>b. Chorus: Why, ah why this fond delay?
</dd></dl>
</dd></dl>
<dl><dt><ul><li>Part 2
</li></ul>
</dt><dd>Recitative: Short is my way (Pleasure, Hercules)
</dd><dd>10. Aria: Yet, can I hear that dulcet lay (Hercules)
</dd><dd>11. Aria: Enjoy the sweet Elysian grove (an Attendant of Pleasure's)
</dd><dd>Recitative: Oh! whither, Reason, dost thou fly? (Hercules)
</dd><dd>12. Trio: Where shall I go? (Pleasure, Virtue, Hercules)
</dd><dd>13. Accompagnato: Mount, mount the steep ascent! (Virtue)
</dd><dd>14. Aria: Mount, mount the steep ascent (Virtue)
</dd><dd>15. Chorus: Arise, arise!
</dd><dd>Recitative: The sounds breathe fire celestial (Hercules)
</dd><dd>16. Aria: Lead, Goddess, lead the way (Hercules)
</dd><dd>17. Chorus: Virtue will place thee in that blest abode
</dd></dl>
</td></tr>
