<th><span class="mh555">Movements/Sections</span><span class="ms555">Mov'ts/Sec's</span>
</th>
<td>2 parts
<dl><dt><ul><li>Part I
</li></ul>
</dt><dd>1. Recitative: Now there arose a new King
</dd><dd>2. Solo and Double Chorus
<dl><dd>a. Solo: And the children of Israel sighed (Alto)
</dd><dd>b. Double Chorus: And their cry came up to God
</dd></dl>
</dd><dd>3. Recitative: Then sent he Moses (Tenor)
</dd><dd>4. Chorus: They loathed to drink of the river
</dd><dd>5. Aria: Their land brought forth frogs (Alto)
</dd><dd>6. Double Chorus: He spake the word
</dd><dd>7. Double Chorus: He gave them hailstones
</dd><dd>8. Chorus: He sent a thick darkness
</dd><dd>9. Chorus: He smote all the first-born of Egypt
</dd><dd>10. Chorus: But for his people
</dd><dd>11. Chorus: Egypt was glad
</dd><dd>12. Double Chorus: He rebuked the Red Sea
</dd><dd>13. Chorus: He led them through the deep
</dd><dd>14. Chorus: But the waters overwhelmed
</dd><dd>15. Double Chorus: And Israel saw that great work
</dd><dd>16. Chorus: And believed the Lord
</dd></dl>
<dl><dt><ul><li>Part II
</li></ul>
</dt><dd>17. Double Chorus: Moses and the children of Israel
</dd><dd>18. Double Chorus: I will sing unto the Lord
</dd><dd>19. Duet: The Lord is my strength (2 Sopranos)
</dd><dd>20. Double Chorus: He is my God
</dd><dd>21. Chorus: And I will exalt Him
</dd><dd>22. Duet: The Lord is a man of war (2 Basses)
</dd><dd>23. Double Chorus: The depths have covered them
</dd><dd>24. Double Chorus: Thy right hand, O Lord
</dd><dd>25. Double Chorus: And in the greatness
</dd><dd>26. Double Chorus: Thou sentest forth Thy wrath
</dd><dd>27. Chorus: And with the blast of Thy nostrils
</dd><dd>28. Aria: The enemy said (Tenor)
</dd><dd>29. Aria: Thou didst blow (Soprano)
</dd><dd>30. Double Chorus: Who is like unto Thee
</dd><dd>31. Double Chorus: The earth swallow'd them
</dd><dd>32. Duet: Thou in Thy mercy (Alto, Tenor)
</dd><dd>33. Double Chorus: The people shall hear
</dd><dd>34. Aria: Thou shalt bring them in (Alto)
</dd><dd>35. Double Chorus: The Lord shall reign
</dd><dd>36. Recitative: For the horse of Pharaoh (Tenor)
</dd><dd>37. Double Chorus: The Lord shall reign
</dd><dd>38. Recitative: And Miriam, the prophetess (Tenor)
</dd><dd>39. Solo Soprano and Double Chorus: Sing ye to the Lord
</dd></dl>

</li><li>Exodus (Part I)&#160;: Composed draft between 15 - 20 October 1738. Completed the filling out of Part I on 28 October 1738.
</li></ul>
