<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<ul><li>From this and the <i><a href="/wiki/Concerto_in_F_major,_HWV_335b_(Handel,_George_Frideric)" title="Concerto in F major, HWV 335b (Handel, George Frideric)">Concerto in F major, HWV 335b</a></i> Handel took some material for his <i><a href="/wiki/Music_for_the_Royal_Fireworks,_HWV_351_(Handel,_George_Frideric)" title="Music for the Royal Fireworks, HWV 351 (Handel, George Frideric)">Music for the Royal Fireworks, HWV 351</a></i>.
</li><li>Grove Music cites both HWV 335a and 335b as "ca.1746, version of ov. to Fireworks Music".
</li><li>Grove Music stipulates the basso continuo as being with organ.
</li></ul>
