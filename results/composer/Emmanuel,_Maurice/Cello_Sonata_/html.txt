<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>"Opus 2" in some sources, e.g. the Ensemble Stanislas recording of some of Emmanuel's chamber music (cello sonata op.2, Sonate en trio Op.11, quartet Op.8, bugle sonata Op.29.) Op.3 in others.
</p><p>Tonal/modal B minor (one sharp in the outer movements...)
</p>
