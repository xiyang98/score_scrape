<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<ul><li>Dubois apparently wanted to keep all options open:<br />"Cette partie de Clarinette pourra remplacer celle de Violoncelle gravée sur la partition." (Notice at the bottom of the clarinet part).<br />And vice versa, on the first page of the full score:<br />"Une partie de Clarinette gravée à part pourra remplacer la partie de Violoncelle."<br />The double bass appears only in the (full) score, but not on the title page. It is needed only if the organ is played without pedal (16 '), or if a harmonium is used.
</li></ul>
<dl><dd>There is also an Ave verum in F major by Dubois published by Graff as part of a collection in 1878 ("Nouvelle collection de douze motets et une messe breve...").
</dd></dl>
