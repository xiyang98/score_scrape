<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Possibly published 1907/1908, at least the Entrée, as this may be the same as-- Publication noted in the <i><a href="/wiki/Bibliographie_de_la_France" title="Bibliographie de la France">Bibliographie de la France</a></i>&#160;2e série, 97e année, Nº 1, 3 Janvier 1908, p.9, r.12.<br />
The official <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://www.theodoredubois.com/catalogue#anchor25">Théodore Dubois</a> website gives 1887 as the year of publication.
