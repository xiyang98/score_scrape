<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>This is no doubt from a larger work of Guerini's (if it is in fact by him at all) - the tagging for cello and piano is tentative, Guerini's instrumental works are apparently mostly for violin duet and for violin and piano , and seem to be multi-movement- it does not seem to belong to any of the works we have&#160;? nor any of those listed by RISM.
</p><p>Published as solos, not "sonatas". Published as Six solos for a violoncello with a thorough bass for the harpsichord, Opus IX, in London ca. 1765.
