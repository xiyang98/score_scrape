<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Dutch Royal Library estimates 1817 as first publication date for the 3 opus numbers immediately preceding (opp. 139 to 141, 3 string quartets, plates 2694 to 2696.). ca.1817 seems a reasonable though hardly proven guess here (the plate number is not that close, admittedly.) OTOH 1818 is the known date for plate 2761 (see <a href="/wiki/Tobias_Haslinger" title="Tobias Haslinger">Tobias Haslinger</a>.)
</p>
