Likewise the triangle notes included in small-notes in the timpani part, though they may have been expected to play from the timpani part?<br /> Since these parts are incomplete, they aren't usable for performance as is, probably, but LoC contains what (probably, based on the interestingly different plate number 230-12?) is the same clarinet part together with a piano part (perhaps 8 pages), also published (based on the André?) by Cundy.
