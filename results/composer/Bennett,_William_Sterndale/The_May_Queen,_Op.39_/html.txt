<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Orchestration of overture according to vocal score: flutes, oboes, clarinets, bassoons, horns, trumpets, trombones, timpani, strings.
</p><p><span class="plainlinks"><a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://anno.onb.ac.at/cgi-content/anno-buch?apm=0&amp;aid=1000001&amp;bd=0001861&amp;teil=0203&amp;seite=00000073&amp;zoom=1">Hofmeister's <i>Monatsbericht</i> (1861), p.73</a></span> (full score, published by Kistner (with translation by Sophie Klingmann; this version - or rather a contemporary ms copy?- is available online from Hathitrust. Given Op.39.) Usually described as a cantata.
</p><p>Libretto has been digitized by BSB. - Schissel
</p>
