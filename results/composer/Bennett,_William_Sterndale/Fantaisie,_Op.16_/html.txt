<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Schumann found the work 'ringing with lovely melodies as over-richly as a nest of nightingales', and reciprocated by dedicating his Etudes Symphoniques Op.13 to Bennett, also published in 1837. 
</p><p>Published by Wessel &amp; Stapleton of London in 1842, by the way. (Work was in print for 8 years in Germany, for 3 years in England, with no public performance? Odd.) - ES
</p>
