<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>There is a String Quintet No.1 (written for quartet and violotta, but playable by string quartet plus violoncello instead like this) in A major from 1897 (WoO 25). (The one recording to date (December 2010) in its original instrumentation pairs the first quintet with Krug's <a href="/wiki/Preis-Sextett,_Op.68_(Krug,_Arnold)" title="Preis-Sextett, Op.68 (Krug, Arnold)">sextet opus 68</a> published 1897.) There are (as of 3/2017) two recordings of the 2nd quintet (this one) but one may be limited in distribution; the other is on cpo.
</p>
