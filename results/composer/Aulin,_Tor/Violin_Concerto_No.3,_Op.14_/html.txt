<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Unusually for a concerto even in 1904 (less unusually for a symphony by then, but perhaps unusually for a concerto) the finale begins and ends in G major, not the home tonic of the work (C minor).
</p>
