<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Sonatas IV and VI employ <i>scordatura</i>, or non-standard violin tunings to accomodate double or triple stoppings that would be unplayable or difficult in the standard tuning.
</p>
