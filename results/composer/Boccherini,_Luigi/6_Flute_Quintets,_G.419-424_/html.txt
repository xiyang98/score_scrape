<td>From the uploader's library. Editor's preface removed. There is no fundamental difference in the editorial approach for this edition compared to the Haas edition below. In the sample flute page for instance, the original D (for <i>dolce</i>) is entered unnecessarily in smaller type as <i>P dolce</i>. Appoggiaturas are realized correctly and in full, and editorial intervention across the board is restrained, which is, after all, a good thing.
