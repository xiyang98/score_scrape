<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Op.61 in Boccherini's autograph catalogue (second version); published under the opus number of the composer.
</p><p>Both versions use basso continuo (that in the 2nd version may be optional. Certainly the recording of the version of 1800 on Hyperion uses b.c.)
</p>
