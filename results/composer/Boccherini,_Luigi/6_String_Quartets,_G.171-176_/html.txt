<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>The quartet G.173 originally began with a Largo (as a slow introduction to the Allegro) omitted in all the printed editions. For a score of the Largo see C. Speck, Boccherinis Streichquartette, Mnchen 1987, 94-95.
</p>
