<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Does anyone have a first edition of this score? The Diabelli works listed in Whistling (1828) under Prludien are diverse but contain none with opus numbers (those in HMB, for 1829 on, start with op. 129 or so, so first publication date may? have preceded that. Also want to determine original instrumentation more surely; even works always played with a certain instrumentation may have been composed for another.)- Schissel
</p><p>(Published as 7 Preludes progressives, possibly by Falter, sometime around 18171819, and according to Google indeed published for the guitar.)
</p>
