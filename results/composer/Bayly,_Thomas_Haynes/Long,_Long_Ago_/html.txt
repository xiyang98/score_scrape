<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<ul><li>Unclear whether Bayly is the composer here or librettist only.

</li><li>There is precedence for Charles Edward Horn arranging a song of Bayly's (see <a href="/wiki/Are_There_Tidings%3F_(Bayly,_Thomas_Haynes)" title="Are There Tidings? (Bayly, Thomas Haynes)"><i>Are There Tidings?</i></a> )
</li></ul>
