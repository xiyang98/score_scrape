<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Unusual concertante (slightly solo in some places, as at the beginning of the finale, but not "concerto") part for pianoforte. Actually a birthday present for his wife Emma Sophia Hartmann, but dedicated on the score to Julius Rietz.
</p>
