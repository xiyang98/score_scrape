<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>This ouverture <i>Imellem Fjeldene</i> (Between the Mountains) by  Niels W. Gade (1788-1854) is typeset from handwritten parts available at Statens Musikbibliotek, Stockholm, Sweden. No score was available.
</p><p>A few obvious misprints and inconsistencies between the parts have been corrected. This is indicated with comments in the different source files.
</p><p>The piece was composed 1850 as an overture for the comedy <i>Imellem  Fjeldene</i> by the Danish poet <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://www.example.com">Carsten Hauch</a> (1792-1872). In the Andante introduction, Gade uses a melody from the collection <a href="/wiki/Folke-Sange_og_Melodier_(Berggreen,_Andreas_Peter)" title="Folke-Sange og Melodier (Berggreen, Andreas Peter)">Norske Folke-Sange og Melodier</a> by the Danish composer <a href="/wiki/Category:Berggreen,_Andreas_Peter" title="Category:Berggreen, Andreas Peter">Andreas Peter Berggreen</a> (1801-1880). The song has a Norwegian text <i>Heimreise fraa Sæteren</i> by <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Edvard_Storm" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Edvard Storm">Edvard Storm</a> (1749-1794). The second theme of the Allegro part might very well come from the same collection, namely the song <i>Saag du nokke Kjærringa mi</i>.
</p>
