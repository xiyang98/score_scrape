<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<ul><li>In the original publication, the subtitle is <i>Smaa Claveerstykke</i>, and there are only words to 16 bars of Jule-Klokkerne (Andantino, Barn Jesus i en Krybbe laa).  Further, Grove Music lists this as a piano piece.  Although Gade may not have intended it to be sung as such, it has been tagged as both piano pieces, and a vocal work.
</li></ul>
