<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Published in orchestral form in 1893 by Enoch frères et Costallat. Publication noted in the <i><a href="/wiki/Bibliographie_de_la_France" title="Bibliographie de la France">Bibliographie de la France</a></i>&#160;2e année, 82e année, Nº 30, 29 Juillet 1893, p.489, r.3456. (And later in 1895 by Litolff, in orchestral or piano form- same HMB month for both.)
</p><p>Given Op.153 in at least one source.
</p><p>An arrangement for military band by Rouveirolis, and a song-arrangement by the composer(? or someone else?) with text by Lafrique, are both mentioned in the 1892 Bibliographie.
</p><p>For piano solo: Publication noted in the <i><a href="/wiki/Bibliographie_de_la_France" title="Bibliographie de la France">Bibliographie de la France</a></i>&#160;2e série, 79e année, Nº 1, 4 Janvier 1890, p. 6, r. 21. (BdlF 1890, p.605: piano duet version.)
</p>
