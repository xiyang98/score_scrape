<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<h4> <span class="mw-headline" id="Extra_Information">Extra Information</span></h4>
<p>Seven pieces belong to this opus in the violin/piano version (which appears to be the original), but the published piano comprise only six in any given collection, by omitting either <i>Étude-arabesque</i> (No.4) or <i>En partant</i> (No.3). 
The sequence shown below follows the violin/piano score, with numbers in brackets representing the sequence in Bessel's and Leduc's respective piano editions that each combined Op.20 with Op.39 as <i>18 Miniatures</i>:
</p>
<ol><li> <i>Marionettes espagnoles</i> (C major)<br />[= Bessel &amp; Leduc 18/13]
</li><li> <i>Romanzetta</i> (D major)<br />[= Bessel 18/18, Leduc 18/17, transposed to D<span class="music-symbol" style="font-family: Arial Unicode MS, Lucida Sans Unicode; font-size:110%">&#x266d;</span> major]
</li><li> <i>En partant</i> (E<span class="music-symbol" style="font-family: Arial Unicode MS, Lucida Sans Unicode; font-size:110%">&#x266d;</span> major)<br />[= Leduc 18/18, omitted by Bessel]
</li><li> <i>Arabesque</i> (D major)<br />[= Bessel 18/13 (as <i>Étude-arabesque</i>), omitted by Leduc]
</li><li> <i>Au berceau</i> (G major)<br />[= Bessel 18/16, Leduc 18/15 (as <i>Berceuse</i>)]
</li><li> <i>Feuille d'album</i> (E<span class="music-symbol" style="font-family: Arial Unicode MS, Lucida Sans Unicode; font-size:110%">&#x266d;</span> major)<br />[= Bessel &amp; Leduc 18/14]
</li><li> <i>Petite marche</i> (D major)<br />[= Bessel 18/17, Leduc 18/16 (as <i>Marche-étude</i>)]
</li></ol>
