<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>This is a children's opera.  It was published during the composer's lifetime only in piano-vocal score by the journal Светлячок (<i>Svetlyachok</i> = <i>The Firefly</i>); if the composer left an orchestration, this would need to be investigated.
The Soviet edition of 1961 reworks the libretto to include spoken dialogue.
