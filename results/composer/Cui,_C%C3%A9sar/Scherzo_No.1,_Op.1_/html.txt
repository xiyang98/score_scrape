<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>The two main themes in this work — B-A-B-E-G (i.e. B<span class="music-symbol" style="font-family: Arial Unicode MS, Lucida Sans Unicode; font-size:110%">&#x266d;</span>-A-B<span class="music-symbol" style="font-family: Arial Unicode MS, Lucida Sans Unicode; font-size:110%">&#x266d;</span>-E-G) and C-C — are taken from Cui's wife's maiden name (Bamberg) and Cui's own initials, respectively.
</p>
