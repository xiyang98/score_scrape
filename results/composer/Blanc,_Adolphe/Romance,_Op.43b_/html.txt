<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>An arrangement of the Romance (second movement) of Adolphe Blanc's Sonata, Op.43, for horn (or english horn) and piano. 
</p>
<dl><dd>Op.44 was published in 1862, and perhaps Op.43 at least was first published around the same time. Op.43b- don't know. - Schissel
</dd></dl>
