<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<ul><li>As d'Indy points out in his Franck biography, Op.12 was earlier assigned, (in 1844), to Franck's 2nd fantasia (on Gulistan).
</li><li>The title <i>Messe solennelle</i> belongs to Mass, FWV 59, though it is mistakenly used with this one.
</li><li>The original version was with orchestral accompaniment, the published version (1872) is an arrangement, though since he never published the original, one might consider it a revision.  Grove Music says "later reduced", not "arranged".
</li><li>The original version also had <i>O salutaris</i> substituted in the published version by the newly composed <i>Panis angelicus</i>. - AllMusic
</li></ul>
