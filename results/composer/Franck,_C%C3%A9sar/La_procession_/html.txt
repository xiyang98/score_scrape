<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<ul><li>BdlF refers to Procession as a mélodie, while RISM refers to it as a sacred song.  While not sacred in the sense of use in a church, the text is about God and nature. Tagged as both.
</li></ul>
