<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<ul><li>Composition and first performance dates from Chandos <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="https://www.chandos.net/products/catalogue/CHAN%209742">9742</a>.
</li><li>The first version of this trio consisted of four movements. After Liszt's advice Franck extracted the final <i>Allegro</i>, which he reworked into a self-standing <i><a href="/wiki/Piano_Trio,_Op.2_(Franck,_C%C3%A9sar)" title="Piano Trio, Op.2 (Franck, César)">Piano Trio</a></i>, Op.2.
</li></ul>
