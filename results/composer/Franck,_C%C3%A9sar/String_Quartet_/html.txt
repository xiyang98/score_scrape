<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Piano duet arrangement by <a href="/wiki/Category:Samazeuilh,_Gustave" title="Category:Samazeuilh, Gustave">Gustave Samazeuilh</a> (1877–1967) published by Hamelle in 1906 or 1907 (see <a href="/wiki/Bibliographie_de_la_France" title="Bibliographie de la France">Bibliographie de la France</a>, 1907, p.9.)
</p><p>The first movement is in D minor with a D major introduction, ending in D major (rather like d'Indy's first string quartet except that the latter's first movement further ends in D minor.) (Possibly premiered by the Ysaÿe Quartet but not I think dedicated to them-- instead dedicated to Léon Reynier, see <span class="plainlinks"><a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://data.bnf.fr/14434433/cesar_franck_musique_de_chambre/">BNF data</a></span> - ES)
</p>
