<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>One of the most popular and financially-successful songs ever published. Used by Kern and Hammerstein in their musical, <i>Show Boat</i> (1927).
</p><p>(4 minutes 54 in the eponymous recording ("After the Ball plus highlights from 'Vaudeville'") made by Joan Morris and William Bolcom for Nonesuch Records and first released <span class="plainlinks"><a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://bolcomandmorris.com/index.php?contentID=1158">on a 1974 LP</a></span>.)
</p><p>Published by Schott in 1917 or 1918 in a violin/piano arrangement, "Nach dem Ball: Blumenwalzer für Violine mit Pianoforte", rev. by Hugo Hartmann (see <span class="plainlinks"><a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://anno.onb.ac.at/cgi-content/anno-buch?apm=0&amp;aid=1000001&amp;bd=0001918&amp;teil=0203&amp;seite=00000004&amp;zoom=1">Hofmeister's <i>Monatsbericht</i> (1918), p.4</a></span>).
</p>
