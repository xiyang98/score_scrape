<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<dl><dt>Carulli's Guitar Methods
</dt></dl>
<ul><li> Op.27 = <i>Méthode complète</i> (ca.1811)
</li><li> Op.61 = <i>Premiere suite a la methode</i> (a supplement to the <i>Méthode complète</i>)
</li><li> Op.71 = <i>Deuxieme suite a la methode</i> (a supplement to the <i>Méthode complète</i>)
</li><li> Op.192 = <i>Supplement a la methode</i> (“The First Year of Guitar Study”, Op.192; another supplement to the <i>Methode complete</i> which includes an etude in octaves cast in a fully-developed sonata-allegro form)
</li><li> Op.241 = <i>École de guitare</i> (a revision of the <i>Méthode complète</i>, Op.27 in polyphonic notation in which, amongst other things, Carulli has rethought his approach to right-hand fingering, ca.1825)
</li></ul>
<dl><dt>Other
</dt></dl>
<ul><li> Op.293 - Méthode complète pour le Dedacorde<br />
</li><li> Op.195 - Solfège et vocalises avec acompagnement de guitare<br />
</li><li> L'Harmonie Appliquée à la Guitare<br />
</li></ul>
