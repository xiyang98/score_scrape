See <span class="plainlinks"><a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://books.google.com/books?id=UgGBXwpHTe8C&amp;pg=PA19">L'Avant-garde musicale et ses sociÃ©tÃ©s Ã  Paris de 1871 Ã  1939</a></span> (Duchesneau, 1997, page 19). </p><p>Alary, according to BNF Library's catalog, seems to have also written a string quintet (fantaisie, pub.1897, op.43) and several other string quartets (no.6, 1917, Paris: C. Hayet, and an unnumbered? Op.60, 1902? published by Durdilly). Worldcat lists a piano quintet Op.45 published by Durdilly and another Op.71.
