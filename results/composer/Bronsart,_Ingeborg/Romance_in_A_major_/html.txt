<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>BSB gives this the title "Romanze A" - somewhat like that which it has in the Sulzer catalog on the page between the score and part. The different shade used for the A- for A-dur/A major - does imply that this is a shorthand way of writing Romanze, A-dur, however.-Schissel
</p>
