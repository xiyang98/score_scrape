<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>This song, attributed to Anne Boleyn as she was awaiting execution in the Tower of London, was transcribed by Arnold Dolmetsch in 1898 from a lute tablature version published in 1611. The current version was published in Dolmetch's book <i>Select English Songs and Dialogues of the 16th and 17th Centuries</i> (Boosey, 1898)
</p>
