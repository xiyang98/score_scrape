<h3> <span class="mw-headline" id="Section_Listing">Section Listing</span></h3>
<ul><li><b>No 1 Symphonia</b>
</li><li><b>No 2 Historicus</b> (Cantus): <i>Cum repleta esset Ninive iniquitate</i>
</li><li><b>No 3 Deus</b> (Bassus): <i>‘Surge, surge, Jona’</i>
</li><li><b>No 4 Historicus</b> (Cantus): <i>Audivit Jonas vocem Domini</i>
</li><li><b>No 5 Historicus</b> (Altus): <i>Et cum processisset in mare</i>
</li><li><b>No 6 Historicus</b> (Chorus geminus): <i>Et proeliabantur venti</i>
</li><li><b>No 7 Nautae</b> (Chorus primus): <i>‘Dii magni! Dii fortes!’</i>
</li><li><b>No 8 Historicus</b> (Cantus): <i>Jonas autem in interioribus navis</i>
</li><li><b>No 9 Gubernator</b> (Altus): <i>‘Quod tu sopore demprimeris?’</i>
</li><li><b>No 10 Nautae</b> (Chorus primus): <i>‘Venite, venite’</i>
</li><li><b>No 11 Historicus</b> (Tenor): <i>Miserunt ergo sortem</i>
</li><li><b>No 12 Nautae</b> (Chorus primus): <i>‘Indica nobis’</i>
</li><li><b>No 13 Jonas</b> (Tenor): <i>‘Hebraeus ego sum’</i>
</li><li><b>No 14 Nautae</b> (Chorus primus): <i>‘Quid faciemus tibi’</i>
</li><li><b>No 15 Jonas</b> (Tenor): <i>‘Tollite me et mittite in mare!’</i>
</li><li><b>No 16 Historicus</b> (Chorus geminus): <i>Tulerunt nautae Jonam</i>
</li><li><b>No 17 Historicus</b> (Bassus): <i>Et praeparavit Dominus cetum grandem</i>
</li><li><b>No 18 Jonas</b> (Tenor): <i>‘Justus es, Domine’</i>
</li><li><b>No 19 Historicus</b> (Chorus primus): <i>Et imperavit Dominus pisci</i>
</li><li><b>No 20 Historicus</b> (Cantus): <i>Et crediderunt Ninivitae</i>
</li><li><b>No 21 Ninivitae</b> (Chorus geminus): <i>‘Peccavimus, Domine’</i>
</li></ul>
