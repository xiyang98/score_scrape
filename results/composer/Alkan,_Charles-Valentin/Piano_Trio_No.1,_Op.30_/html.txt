<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Alkan wrote a Piano Trio No.2 sometime in the 1840s, but it is lost, along with some string quintets and sextets and his Symphony in B minor. None of these works were published.
</p>
