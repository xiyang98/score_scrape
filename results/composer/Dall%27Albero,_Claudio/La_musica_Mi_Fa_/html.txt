</div><div class="jq-ui-tabs hide-until-loaded" id="tabScore2"><h3 class="nojs"> <span class="mw-headline" id="Texts">Texts</span></h3>
<p><i>Italian</i><br />
<b>I strofa</b><br />
Quando ascolto il canto di una dolce melodia…<br />
Quando in un cartone mi emoziona una canzone…<br />
Quando un pianoforte riempie l’aria di armonia…<br />
Quando incalza il ritmo di strumenti a percussione<br />
con un loop di ‘stappi-stù, stappi-stù’…<br />
È allora che la musica mi fa… mi fa… mi fa…<br />
</p><p><b>II strofa</b><br />
Quando una canzone non vorrei che avesse fine…<br />
Quando in un concerto c’è una bella sinfonia…<br />
Quando un flauto suona scale, arpeggi e volatine…<br />
Quando sento insieme contrabbasso e batteria<br />
con un loop di ‘stappi-stù, stappi-stù’…<br />
È allora che la musica mi fa… mi fa… mi fa…<br />
</p><p><b>Ritornello</b><br />
Sognare di stare in un bosco incantato,<br />
Volare con Goku nel cielo stellato,<br />
Danzare in un ballo di gnomi e di maghi,<br />
Riempire di coccole piccoli draghi.<br />
Andare a cavallo su un bianco centauro,<br />
Giocare a pallone con un dinosauro,<br />
Tuffarmi con Tarzan, con Chita e il mio cane,<br />
Scoprire tesori in terre lontane.<br />
</p><p>La musica solo può far la magia<br />
Di far galoppare la mia fantasia. <br />
</p><p><br />
<i>English</i><br />
<b>Verse I</b><br />
When I hear the singing of a sweet melody...<br />
When I am moved by a song in a cartoon…<br />
When a piano fills the air with harmony…<br />
When the rhythm of percussion instruments is pressing<br />
with a loop of ‘stappi-stù, stappi-stù’…<br />
At that moment the music ‘mi fa’… ‘mi fa’… ‘mi fa’…<br />
</p><p><b>Verse II</b><br />
When there’s a song that I wish wouldn’t end…<br />
When in a concert there’s a great symphony…<br />
When a flute plays scales, arpeggios and little flights…<br />
When I hear bass and drums together<br />
with a loop  of ‘stappi-stù, stappi-stù’…<br />
At that moment the music ‘mi fa’… ‘mi fa’… ‘mi fa’…<br />
</p><p><b>Refrain</b><br />
Dreaming of being in an enchanted forest,<br />
Flying with Goku in the starry sky,<br />
Dancing in a dance of gnomes and wizards,<br />
Giving little dragons lots of cuddles.<br />
Riding on the back of a white centaur,<br />
Playing football with a dinosaur,<br />
Diving with Tarzan, with Chita and my dog,<br />
Discovering treasures in distant lands.<br />
</p><p>Music alone can make the magic<br />
To set my imagination galloping.<br /><span class="na-marker" id="na-tabScore2" data-type="score" data-name="Texts" data-tabid="tabScore2"></span>
</p>

<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<h3> <span class="mw-headline" id="Voicing">Voicing</span></h3>
<hr />
<p>The voices are divided in six staves, but sometimes the female voices are further divided in Soprano 1 and 2 and Contralto 1 (mezzosoprano) and 2 (real contralto). However, in these two vocal parts divided, the differences are so small as not to add two more staves in the score.
</p>
<h3> <span class="mw-headline" id="History"> History</span></h3>
<hr />
<p><i>La musica Mi Fa...</i> is a song composed by Claudio Dall’Albero, starting out from an idea by Gregorio Mazzarese, with the aim of creating a bridge between classical music and the world of children.
The composition has the traditional structure of a song, alternating verses and chorus. Commissioned in 2002 by the Academy of Santa Cecilia, it has been taught to school children in Rome who have joined the initiative. It was an experiment never tried before in Italy.
</p>
<h3> <span class="mw-headline" id="Instructions_for_use"> Instructions for use </span></h3>
<hr />
<p>All published material is free. Teachers interested in this project will be able to download the score for two voices and piano, and the song in mp3 format. The audio files are available in two versions:
</p>
<ul><li> The first one, complete with vocal parts, allows you to learn the melodies;
</li><li> The second, purely instrumental version, can be used as a 'basis' for the kids to study and learn the song.
</li></ul>
<p>The verses are almost always for just one voice; only afterwards is it joined by the second voice, which continues throughout the chorus.<br />
The song can also be enjoyed by adult audiences, whether as music, or as a way of gaining familiarity with the Italian language.<br />
The score for six-voice choir can be performed by a professional choir or by a good amateur choir. The orchestral score, which can also be downloaded, has the same instrumentation as the recording of the audio files.<br />
The orchestration can therefore be used not only for the polyphonic choral version for six voices but also for the two-voice version, as in the audio files.<br />
You can request the orchestral parts directly from the composer at <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="mailto:md8168@mclink.it">this address</a>
</p><p><br />
</p>
