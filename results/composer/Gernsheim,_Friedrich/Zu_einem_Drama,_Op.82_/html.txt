<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>From Philip Hale's <span class="plainlinks"><a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://books.google.com/books?id=fDkvAQAAIAAJ&amp;pg=PA1019">extensive notes</a></span> to the January 27/28 1911 Boston premiere (conducted by Max Fiedler) , was first? performed in Berlin October 24 1910. (These notes also contain premiere dates for a large number of other Gernsheim compositions, as far as were obtainable by Mr. Hale at the time, and seem fairly useful within their limitations; linked to on the category page.)
</p><p>There is an article on Zu einem Drama by Carl Fuchs in NZM, 79 Jahrg., pp.283-86, 16 May 1912 issue. (Seems to claim that the premiere was in Dortmund in October 1910 though.)
</p>
