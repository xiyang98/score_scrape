<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Note: "The Chorus can either be sung or left out."
</p><p>From one advertisement: 'Just the song for our girls to sing, and every body else's girls will be delighted with it.<i>Fort Wayne Sentinel</i>'.  'This song is very popular in the East.  We heartily recommend it to "Our Girls".<i>Massillon News</i>'.
</p>
