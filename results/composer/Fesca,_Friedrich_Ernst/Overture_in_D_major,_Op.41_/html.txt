<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Parts to this overture (which has been recorded, on cpo in 2007) are in the possession of the Royal Library of the Netherlands and at the French National Library.
</p><p>Publication of Op.41 instrumental parts by N. Simrock in Bonn was mentioned in the March 1826 AMZ Intelligenz-Blatt.
</p><p>First publication by Farrenc: see <span class="plainlinks"><a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://books.google.com/books?id=nhk6AAAAIAAJ&amp;pg=PA326">Journal général d'annonces des œuvres de musique, gravures</a></span>, volume 1, 28 October 1825 issue, p.326. Also, see BdlF as above (referred to as Op.41e).
</p>
