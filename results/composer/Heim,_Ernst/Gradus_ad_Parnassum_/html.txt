<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="General_Information">General Information</span></h2>
<div class="wi_body" style="width:100%">
<table border="0">

<tr>
<th class="wi_head">Work Title
</th>
<td class="wi_head" style="width:100%">Gradus ad Parnassum
</td></tr>
<tr>
<th class="wi_head">Alt<span class="mh555">ernative</span><span class="ms555">.</span> Title
</th>
<td class="wi_head">a collection of violin studies in progressive order
</td></tr>
<tr>
<th>Composer
</th>
<td><a href="/wiki/Category:Heim,_Ernst" title="Category:Heim, Ernst">Heim, Ernst</a>
</td></tr>
<tr>
<th><span class="mh555">I-Catalogue Number</span><span class="ms555">I-Cat. No.</span>
</th>
<td>IEH 2
</td></tr>
<tr>
<th><span class="mh555">Movements/Sections</span><span class="ms555">Mov'ts/Sec's</span>
</th>
<td>10 books of studies:
<ul><li>Book 1. First position. Elementary studies in the most practicable major keys --
</li><li>Book 2. First position: More advanced studies in major and minor keys&#160;; Early double stopping&#160;; Enharmonic change. Half position: Changing from 1st to half position --
</li><li>Book 3. First position. Chords and easy arpeggios&#160;; Chromatic passages. Second position: Changing from 1st to 2nd position 
</li><li>Book 4. Third position: Changing between 1st and 3rd positions&#160;; Portamento and tremolo 
</li><li>Book 5. Fourth position: Changing between the 1st and 4th positions. Fifth position: changing between the 1st and 5th positions --
</li><li>Book 6. Sixth position: Changing beween the 1st and 6th positions. Seventh position: Changing between the 1st and 7th positions --
</li><li>Book 7. Eighth to twelfth positions: Studies arranged according to their musico-technical object, independent of position: Studies on the shake and passing sharp --
</li><li>Book 8. Studies on staccato. Studies on springing bow, spiccato, saltato. Studies on octave passages. Studies on double stops and chords --
</li><li>Book 9. Studies on double stops and chords (continuation). Studies on the double shake. Studies on flying staccato, thrown stroke. Studies on arpeggios (difficult). Studies on tenths and other large intervals --
</li><li>Book 10. The polyphonic style: prelude, fugue, fughetta. Concert studies: Harmonics and piccicato (with the left hand). 
</li></ul>
<p><br />
</p>
<ul><li>1. Erste Lage. Elementar-Etüden in den gangbarsten Dur-Tonarten. 
</li><li>2. Erste Lage. Schwierigere Etüden in Dur u. Moll, leichte Doppelgriffe, enharmonische Verwechslung. Halbe Lage. Wechsel zwischen I. u. halber Lage. 
</li><li>3. Erste Lage. Akkorde u. leichte Arpeggien. Chromatische Gänge. Zweite Lage. Wechsel zwischen I. u. II. Lage. 
</li><li>4. Dritte Lage. Wechsel zwischen I. u. III. Lage. Portamento, Beben od. Tremolo. 
</li><li>5. Vierte Lage. Wechsel zwischen I. u. IV. Lage. Fünfte Lage. Wechsel zwischen I. u. V. Lage. 
</li><li>6. Sechste Lage. Wechsel zwischen I. u. VI. Lage. Siebente Lage. Wechsel zwischen I. u. VII. Lage. 
</li><li>7. Achte – zwölfte Lage. Etüden nach ihren musikalisch-technischen Zwecken geordnet, unabhängig v. den Lagen. A. Triller u. Pralltriller-Etüden. –
</li><li>8. – B–E. Etüden f. Staccato, Springbogen, Spiccato, Saltato, Oktaven-Passagen, Doppelgriffe u. Akkorde. 
</li><li>9. Etüden f. Doppelgriffe u. Akkorde (Fortsetzung). Etüden f. Doppeltriller. Etüden f. fliegendes Staccato, geworfenen Strich. Etüden f. Arpeggien (schwierige). Etüden f. Decimen u. andere grosse Spannungen.
</li></ul>
</td></tr>
