<h4> <span class="mw-headline" id="About_the_Kram_Editions">About the Kram Editions</span></h4>
<p>Posted the original version (urtext) and a performance version with a combination of orchestral reduction of the tutti sections and a continuo realization for the solo sections. You can use this to create a complete continuo part if you wish. The parts are on the Parts tab and a version for violin and piano is on the Arrangements and Transcriptions tab. A number of questionable harmonies, but I kept them. There were few places in the score that looked like obvious errors (I made notations in places where notes were changed).
</p>
