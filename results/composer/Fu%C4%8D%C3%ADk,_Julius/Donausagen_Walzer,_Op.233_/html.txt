<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<ul><li>Published by Bosworth &amp; Co. 1910 (B &amp; Co 13480), with the instrumentation of "salon orchestra".
</li><li>In the band arrangement, there is a diacritical, i.e. "Donaŭsagen", but none of the references for the original have the "ŭ", so if an original edition ever gets uploaded to IMSLP where this can be checked, the page title may have to be changed.
</li></ul>
