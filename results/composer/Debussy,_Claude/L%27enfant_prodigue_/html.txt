<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Sacred cantata (Prodigal Son story), not opera, though has been presented so.
</p>
<dl><dd>Actually, it is sometimes described as a cantata, but also (however accurately) as an oratorio or a lyric scene. How did the composer describe it (on score or in correspondence, of which he left a good deal?) - Schissel
</dd></dl>
