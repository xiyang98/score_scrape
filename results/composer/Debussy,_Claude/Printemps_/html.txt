<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Debussy himself later made an arrangement for mixed chorus and piano 4 hands, the 1st movement of which (Tres modéré) was published in 3 supplements in the magazine <i>La Revue Musicale</i>, spring 1904. It was Henri Büsser who, in 1912, re-orchestrated the piece (without voices) from this piano 4 hands version under Debussy's supervision and it was published in 1913.
</p><p>The complete transcription for solo piano by Henri Büsser (pub. Durand, 1930, plate D.&amp;F. 11,929) is currently under copyright worldwide.
</p>
