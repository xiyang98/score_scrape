				<h1 id="firstHeading" class="firstHeading pagetitle page-header">
					Clair de lune (Debussy, Claude)				</h1>	

				<div class="body"><div lang="en" dir="ltr" class="mw-content-ltr"><p>This title can refer to three different works by Debussy:
</p>
<ul><li>The famous piano piece entitled <i>Clair de lune</i> is No.3 of the <i><a href="/wiki/Suite_bergamasque_(Debussy,_Claude)" title="Suite bergamasque (Debussy, Claude)">Suite bergamasque</a></i>
</li><li>A song to text by <a href="/wiki/Category:Verlaine,_Paul" title="Category:Verlaine, Paul">Paul Verlaine</a> is No.3 in the first book of <i><a href="/wiki/F%C3%AAtes_galantes_(Debussy,_Claude)" title="Fêtes galantes (Debussy, Claude)">Fêtes galantes</a></i>
</li><li>An early setting of the same Verlaine text to different music is known here as <i><a href="/wiki/Votre_%C3%A2me_est_un_paysage_choisi_(Debussy,_Claude)" title="Votre âme est un paysage choisi (Debussy, Claude)">Votre âme est un paysage choisi</a></i>
</li></ul>
