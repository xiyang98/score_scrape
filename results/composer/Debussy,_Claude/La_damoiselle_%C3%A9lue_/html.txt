<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<ul><li>Grove Music lists only one soprano soloist, and the score lists only <i>une récitante</i>, p.18. It is unclear why the first performance (and others since then) used two soloists, though it may have something to do with the range of the récitante part.
</li><li>Debussy became familiar with Rossetti's poetry through Sarrazin's <i>Poètes modernes de l'Angleterre</i>, published in the mid-1880s.
</li></ul>
