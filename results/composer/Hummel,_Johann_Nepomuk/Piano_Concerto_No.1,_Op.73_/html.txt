<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>According to Chandos, an arrangement of the Mandolin Concerto of 1799. Also known as a concertino.
</p><p>A later/earlier concerto (#0?) concerto (in C, from 1809 - dedicated to Archduke Rudolph and published ca.1812 by Riedl and later by Haslinger ca.1827 as "Zweytes Concert") (described by Derek Carew as his first mature piano concerto), his concerto Op.34/34a/36 in C, has also been recorded in the Chandos records series (without number however.)
</p>
