<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p><i>may</i> be a translation into English and republication of one of Herman's lieder (even though Thomson's English text is the source), of which there are plenty (see e.g. Hofmeister which lists many sets of lieder by Herman - at least opp. 7, 8, 9, 12 (duet), 14 (chorus), 25, 31, 37  (by this point published in the US), 39,  41 just up to 1900.) Probably not though.
</p>
