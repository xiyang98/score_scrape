<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>This is the set containing the famous 'Felton's Air' or 'Felton's Gavot' - the Andante with variations in Concerto III. A version of the tune is still used in the Lichfield Morris Dance tradition, for the dance called <i>Ring O' Bells</i>.
</p>
