<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>The RISM link only gives the single harpsichord version, but Grove Music lists the 2-harpsichord (or harpsichord and fortepiano) first with a date of 1763, with the single harpsichord version listed afterwards with no date.
