<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>A different version of C.P.E Bach's Harpsichord Concerto, H.425. It has been proposed that this is a later arrangement, possibly not by C.P.E. Bach, of the Harpsichord Concerto but this cannot be proven. Some have argued that both versions are authentic and the Flute version came first.
</p>
