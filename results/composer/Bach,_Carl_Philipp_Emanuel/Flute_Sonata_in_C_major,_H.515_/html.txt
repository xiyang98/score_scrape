<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Allmusic.com has Allegretto / Andantino / no tempo marking&#160;; the ms copy at RISM has Allegretto / Andantino / Allegro. Sometimes called a sonata (and recorded so in Kuijken's album of the composer's complete flute sonatas), sometimes a trio.
</p>
