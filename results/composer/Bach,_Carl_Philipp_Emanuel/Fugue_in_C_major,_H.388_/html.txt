<td>Transposed to D&#160;major. The difference is only that the writing in the second version is more contiguous. In spite of everything, I could not resist the temptation, to immitate some individual motifs on the 2nd guitar, so that a different effect happens, as if only the same guitar is playing this motive. That is just the difference to the original, where there is "only" one harpsichord player. If performed I would look forward toward a small reference to my efforts.
