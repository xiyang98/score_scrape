<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>The earliest publication in solo keyboard form seems to be in 1950 (arranged for piano; first publication in a typeset edition for clavichord/harpsichord is more recent still), but before that there is the 1938 violin/piano arrangement above (assuming this is the same work- compare?). In 1905 Wotquenne (p.25) lists it as still being in manuscript.
</p>
