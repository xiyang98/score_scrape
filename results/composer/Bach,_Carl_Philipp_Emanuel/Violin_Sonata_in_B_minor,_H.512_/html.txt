<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>May have been first published by J. Rieter-Biedermann in a (not very scholarly by modern standards- changing dynamics, articulations, adding notes in another composition (by WF Bach) that he edited...) edition by <a href="/wiki/Category:Brahms,_Johannes" title="Category:Brahms, Johannes">Johannes Brahms</a>, according to a summary of an article by Mauser (1983)) - the edition being published in 1864. (That btw would be <span class="plainlinks"><a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://www.worldcat.org/oclc/731144091">WorldCat</a></span> this edition, published indeed <span class="plainlinks"><a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://anno.onb.ac.at/cgi-content/anno-buch?apm=0&amp;aid=1000001&amp;bd=0001864&amp;teil=0203&amp;seite=00000175&amp;zoom=1">Hofmeister's <i>Monatsbericht</i> (1864), p.175</a></span>.)<br />
Published as a pair with <a href="/wiki/Violin_Sonata_in_C_minor,_H.514_(Bach,_Carl_Philipp_Emanuel)" title="Violin Sonata in C minor, H.514 (Bach, Carl Philipp Emanuel)">Violin Sonata in C minor, H.514</a>
</p>
