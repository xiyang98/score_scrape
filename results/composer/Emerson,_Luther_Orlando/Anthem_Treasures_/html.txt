<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>In standard notation with tonic sol-fa beneath. <br />The same plates were published in London &amp; Glasgow (Bayley &amp; Ferguson) under the title <b>Anthem Treasures</b>, and in Toronto (Hawkes &amp; Harris) under the title <b>The Canadian Anthem Treasury</b>. As both publications are undated and have no plate numbers, it is unclear which version came first.
</p>
