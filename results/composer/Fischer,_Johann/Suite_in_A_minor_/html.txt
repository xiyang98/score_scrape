<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>This work is listed in the Haynes catalog as Suite No.4 of <i>Musicalische Fürstenlust</i>, so a possible instrumentation is
</p>
<dl><dd><dl><dd>Dessus 1 = Oboe 1, Dessus 2 = Oboe 2, Taille = English horn, Bass = Bassoon;
</dd></dl>
</dd></dl>
<p>It seems, that <i>Musicalische Fürstenlust</i> and <i>Tafelmusik</i> (1702) are the same collections with different names. Both consists of 64 movements,
the first 39 for 4 parts and the rest for 3 parts. The 3 part pieces are here at IMSLP as part of the "Tafelmusik". Some changes on the corresponding 
workpages will follow.
</p>
