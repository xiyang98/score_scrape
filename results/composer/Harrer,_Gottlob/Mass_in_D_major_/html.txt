<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>As noted above Berlin (D-B) has an autograph of the first movement (dated 1755, so not an autograph made by the composer in 1735, but still an autograph. Dates on autograph scores are not always the dates of composition for several reasons, one of them being that composers prepare autograph scores/parts of works more than once.)
</p><p>Late 18th century Abschrift (ms-copy) parts are in the Berlin State library.
</p>
