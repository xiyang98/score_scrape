<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>A new edition of the score has been published by Spaeth/Schmid in 2008. The "aus "concentus musico-instrumentalis" 1701" is from their title.<br />One of the two recordings (on the Arcana label, 1998, recorded with the suite Turcaria and two orchestral suites, advertised then as part 1 of a series of Fux' orchestral music) only has 10 sections - either groups of movements are condensed from the 17 listed here (Menuet I - Menuet II (Trio) - Menuet I from 3 to 1 movement, e.g.?) or the piece as recorded is cut/excerpted. (The other existing recording, a 2008 recording on Brilliant Classics, also has 10 sections. Actually, the Arcana recording is preceded by a "Fanfare ex C" so perhaps has 11...) - Schissel
</p>
