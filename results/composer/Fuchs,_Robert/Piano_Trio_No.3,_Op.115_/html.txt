<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Date 1921-2-15 from ONB catalog and "Kf. Schneider 1968." reference (with scan of their library card.)<br />
Since publication of the first edition was announced in the January, 1926 issue of Hofmeister's <i>Musikalisch-literarischer Monatsbericht</i> the work could have been published late in 1925.
</p>
