<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Original songs may here mean not reprints of European editions- the five may have been meant as a group to be published in 1875 by M. Gray. Can find no earlier source yet for e.g. Ich sah&#8217; dich weinen.
</p>
