<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<h4> <span class="mw-headline" id="Version_History">Version History</span></h4>
<ul><li> Composed 1916–17 under the title <i>El corregidor y la molinera</i> as music for a pantomime
</li><li> Revised 1917–19 as a ballet, and retitled <i>El Sombrero de Tres Picos</i>
</li><li> Two suites for orchestra were extracted from the ballet by the composer, 1919–21
</li><li> The complete ballet was also arranged for pianola by the composer, 1921–26
</li></ul>
