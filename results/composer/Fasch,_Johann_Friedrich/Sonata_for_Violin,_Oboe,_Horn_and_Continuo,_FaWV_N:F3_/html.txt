<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>The coverpage lists an oboe d´amore. The parts contain an oboe part and a part for oboe d´amore. But this is not
the usual oboe d´amore in a, but an oboe in B<span class="music-symbol" style="font-family: Arial Unicode MS, Lucida Sans Unicode; font-size:110%">&#x266d;</span>. In the worklist of the Fasch society this sonata is listed as for violin, oboe, horn and continuo.
</p>
