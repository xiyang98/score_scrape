<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Recorded on Chandos CHAN 0783 (described as a premiere recording. The notes to this recording find that the horn parts are virtuoso and that the wind parts take active roles- to paraphrase/infer, perhaps giving this concerto something of the air of a concerto grosso). Unless they were playing Pisendel's arrangement...
</p>
