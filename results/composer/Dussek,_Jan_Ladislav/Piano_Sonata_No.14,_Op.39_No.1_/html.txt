<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Like many of Dussek's sonatas this is in 2 movements (or three in this case, the Andantino going attacca into the Allegro), though in this case with no real movement breaks (at least in the 1961 edition) and an odd, fantasiacal finale
</p>
