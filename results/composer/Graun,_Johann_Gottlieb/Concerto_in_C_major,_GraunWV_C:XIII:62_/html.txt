<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>A reconstruction of the 1st movement is possible. The manuscript ends at a point where the music seems to be about to come to a final cadence into C major before a possible repetition of the opening ritornello. The final bars of the solo episode could be taken from some very similar material at the beginning of pg.20, where the solo instruments cadence into E minor followed by a tutti section in E minor which mirrors very closely the opening few bars of the piece
</p>
