<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Haven't heard this concerto yet but from the score am impressed by it.<br />The NMI archive lists 3 violin concertos (no.3 incomplete?) (532/013-014a,b; also 532/053?) by Hollander- am guessing this is no.1, but just guessing (also, a cello concerto (1913) and a piano concerto and symphony in ms ("Roland", mentioned in Stokes' 1909 encyclopedia)...hrmm...&#160;:) ).
</p><p>Op.15 was republished by Chester in 1922. See <span class="plainlinks"><a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://www.worldcat.org/oclc/77284865">WorldCat</a></span>.<br />
There's also a suite Op.6, a ballade, a double concerto (violin, cello and orchestra) "Idyll" Op.18, a Fantaisie pastorale Op.26 (see <span class="plainlinks"><a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://www.worldcat.org/oclc/498718112">WorldCat</a></span>), etc. among violin and orchestra works. (And a fair number of chamber works etc., songs..., but those seemed most pertinent just here.) -- ES
</p>
