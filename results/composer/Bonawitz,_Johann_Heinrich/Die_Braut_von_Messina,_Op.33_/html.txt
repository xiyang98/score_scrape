<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Alternate title is title of the (unpreviewable) libretto as it appears at books.google.com. One of at least three operas written by Bonawitz. The other two are Ostrolenka (excerpts also scanned in at LOC, including a Polonaise); and Irma, Op.49 (1885).
</p>
