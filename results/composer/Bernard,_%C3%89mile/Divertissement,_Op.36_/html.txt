<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>St. Paul page gives 1894 as composition date. This is <b>certainly</b> not true, since a piano duet reduction was published in 1890 (according to BNF and BdlF). Durand table suggests 1888.
</p>
