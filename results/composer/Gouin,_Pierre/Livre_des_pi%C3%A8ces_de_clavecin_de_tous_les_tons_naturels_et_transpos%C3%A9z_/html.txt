<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>A recurrent note from the copyist «&#160;tirée des pièces de son opera de clavessin&#160;» (excerpt from his works for harpsichord), indicates that the manuscript copy (Bnf MS Rés. 475) is not autograph, but a compilation of pieces for harpsichord, with some arrangements of pieces for viols, by an anonymous hand.
</p>
