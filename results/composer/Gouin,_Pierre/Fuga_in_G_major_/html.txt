<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Although the titles states «per cembalo», the writing is completely compatible with the organ, as show the pedal notes toward the end. However, the pipe organ is rather scarce in Russia during the XVIIIth c. which could explain why it is not mentioned specifically by the copyist hand.
</p>
