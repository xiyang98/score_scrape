<th>Misc. Notes
</th>
<td>Test of the VSL Konzerthaus Organ library. This full library "contains an ample selection of stops from the organ in the large hall of the Konzerthaus in Vienna (built by brothers Rieger in Silesia), resulting in a virtual instrument featuring three manuals and a pedal". Richard Strauss composed his “Festliches Praeludium” for its inauguration in 1913. Pierre Gouin's MIDI file was imported into Cubase7 and duplicated to extract the treble and bass lines to separate parts for greater control. East West Spaces Convolution reverb (Hamburg  Cathedral, 2.8s) was also added.  The versions of the Praeludium use VSL presets which are listed in each version. All versions played on Manual I. Leveling kept consistent with the volume of each preset.
