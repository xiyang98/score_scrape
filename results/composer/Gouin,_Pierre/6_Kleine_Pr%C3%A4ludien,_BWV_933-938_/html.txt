<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Sechs Kleine Præludien,  J. N. Forkel &amp; J. C. Kittel Collection.
</p><p>Are these known with certainty to be by Bach? Apparently all available sources are from after 1750, and contain un-Bach-like keyboard writing and "intervention" (possibly improvements by e.g. C.P.E. Bach) besides (in the opinion of R.D.P. Jones, <span class="plainlinks"><a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://books.google.com/books?id=AzBoAgAAQBAJ&amp;pg=PA241"><i>The Creative Development of Johann Sebastian Bach, Volume II: 1717-1750: Music to Delight the Spirit</i>, volume 2, p.241</a></span> (who does not raise a question of authenticity but does make those other points...) - Schissel
</p><p><br />
</p>
