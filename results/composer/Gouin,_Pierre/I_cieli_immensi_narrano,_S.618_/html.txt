<th>Misc. Notes
</th>
<td>This arrangement has a special (and necessary) violin 2b part, written especially for beginner violinists who have never played in ensembles before. Everything is in the first position, and the rhythms are uncomplicated. The purpose of this arrangement is to allow the novice violinist to be a full participant in higher-level music making.<br />These file(s) are part of the <a href="/wiki/Category:WIMA_files" title="Category:WIMA files">Werner Icking Music Collection</a>.
