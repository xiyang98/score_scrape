<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>The 10 keyboard pieces edited here are taken from a manuscript book kept in the British Museum (London), R. App. 58. They are published in the same order as they appear in the manuscript.
All the pieces are anonymous, except for the second, <i>A Hornepype</i>, attributed to Hugh Aston (ca.1485-1558).
</p>
