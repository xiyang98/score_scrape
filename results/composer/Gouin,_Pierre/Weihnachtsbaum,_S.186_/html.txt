<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/es:Daniela_von_B%C3%BClow" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:es:Daniela von Bülow">Daniela von Bülow</a> was the daughter of Hans von Bülow and Cosima (von Bülow-Wagner), named after her short-lived, recently-deceased brother Daniel (1839-1859).
</p>
<h4> <span class="mw-headline" id="Version_History">Version History</span></h4>
<ul><li> Composed for solo piano, 1873–74 (= 1st version, S.185a)
</li></ul>
<dl><dd> Version not published
</dd></dl>
<ul><li> Nos.1–12 arranged for piano 4 hands, 1875? (= S.612a)
</li></ul>
<dl><dd> Version not published
</dd></dl>
<ul><li> Solo piano versions of Nos.1–12 revised 1874–76 (= 2nd version, S.186)
</li></ul>
<dl><dd> Published 1882 by <a href="/wiki/F%C3%BCrstner" title="Fürstner">Fürstner</a> (Berlin)
</dd></dl>
<ul><li> Piano 4 hands versions of Nos.1–12 revised 1876–81 (= S.613)
</li></ul>
<dl><dd> Published 1882 by <a href="/wiki/F%C3%BCrstner" title="Fürstner">Fürstner</a> (Berlin)
</dd></dl>
<ul><li> Solo piano version of No.7 revised 1879–81? (= S.186/7a)
</li></ul>
<dl><dd> Published 1950 by <a href="/wiki/Schirmer" title="Schirmer">Schirmer</a> (New York)
</dd></dl>
<ul><li> No.2 revised for tenor solo, female chorus and organ or harmonium by Liszt, 1881, as <i><a href="/wiki/O_heilige_Nacht,_S.49_(Liszt,_Franz)" title="O heilige Nacht, S.49 (Liszt, Franz)">O heilige Nacht</a></i>, S.49
</li></ul>
