<th>Extra Information
</th>
<td>Once attributed to J.S. Bach. See <i><a href="/wiki/8_Kleine_Pr%C3%A4ludien_und_Fugen,_BWV_553-560_(Bach,_Johann_Sebastian)" title="8 Kleine Präludien und Fugen, BWV 553-560 (Bach, Johann Sebastian)">8 Kleine Präludien und Fugen, BWV 553-560</a></i>
</td></tr></table>
</div>
<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>"Bach’s <i>Eight Short Preludes and Fugues</i> bwv553–60 have also been attributed to both Tobias and Ludwig [Krebs]; on stylistic grounds neither seems likely." (Hugh J. McLean. "Krebs." In <i>Grove Music Online</i>. Oxford Music Online)
</p>
