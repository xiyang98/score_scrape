<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p><b>Based on music by <a href="/wiki/Category:Schubert,_Franz" title="Category:Schubert, Franz">Franz Schubert</a> (1797-1828).</b>
</p><p>Adaptations:
</p>
