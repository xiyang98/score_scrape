<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>There are two versions of this concerto. 
BOT. 85&#160;: in C minor, with string quintet. No edition, only the manuscrit in Parma.
BOT. 88 et 90&#160;: en B minor, with piano. A lot of edition.
</p><p>The orchestration was taken from Luck's Music Library, but their catalogue listing is arranged by Ludwin. It is not known whether Ludwin altered Bottesini's original orchestration, so this is a guide only.
</p>
