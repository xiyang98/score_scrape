<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>A setting, possibly the same one? , of "Gekommen ist der Maie" by Franz published separately by Mechetti in 1847 (see <span class="plainlinks"><a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://anno.onb.ac.at/cgi-content/anno-buch?apm=0&amp;aid=1000001&amp;bd=0001847&amp;teil=0203&amp;seite=00000085&amp;zoom=1">Hofmeister's <i>Monatsbericht</i> (1847), p.85</a></span>). (Whether or not the two settings are the same - assuming the 1847-published one still survives - might be mentioned in a sufficiently good book on the composer.)
</p>
