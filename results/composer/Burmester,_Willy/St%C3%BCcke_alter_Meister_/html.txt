<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Intermediate level pieces, but they are not arranged in progressive order<br />
The cello version piano score includes a violin stave, notating the cello part an octave higher than sounding. Thus it can be used by a violinist if necessary.<br />
Since some of the pieces (for example Nos. 28 and 30) are in different keys in the violin and cello versions, a separate piano score is needed for performances with the violin.
</p>
