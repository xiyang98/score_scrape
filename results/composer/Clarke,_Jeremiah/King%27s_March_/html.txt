<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<ul><li>Though Grove Music doesn't give a list of Clarke's works other than just his two famous works, this has been recorded under the title "King William's March" for brass ensemble, and apparently for organ as well.
</li><li>This particular typeset is two voices, one in treble clef and the second in bass clef.
</li></ul>
