<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<ul><li>According to someone, this song would only have been notated and arranged by T. Cottrau.<br /> 
</li><li>The 1849 edition has only the Neapolitan lyrics: after translated into Italian this piece was widely much appreciated.<br /> 
</li><li>"Santa Lucia" in the title refers to a borough of Naples, close to the sea side: its name comes from the parish and basilica of Santa Lucia, near Naples.
</li></ul>
