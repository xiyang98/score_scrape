<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Used in the film <i>El dia que me quieras</i>; see also <a href="/wiki/El_dia_que_me_quieras_(Gardel,_Carlos)" title="El dia que me quieras (Gardel, Carlos)">El dia que me quieras (Gardel, Carlos)</a>.
The instrumentation of voice, saxophone, trumpet, violin, double bass, accordion is the earliest on listed in the WorldCat entries, but the companion piece <i>El dia que me quieras</i> is for voice, strings, accordion, piano.  The WorldCat entry might not be the original film version, but then again, there is no reason two different instrumentations couldn't be used at different points in the film.
</p>
