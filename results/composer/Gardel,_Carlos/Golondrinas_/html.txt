<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>It is not certain what this is arranged from.  There is at least one folksong, and at least one popular song based on "La Golondrina", but they don't seem to be the same piece (although folksongs themselves can vary widely depending on region).
</p>
