<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Note on the title: This work very most likely celebrates the svastika, as a fortunate or Sun symbol (as described in the early parts of the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Swastika" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Swastika">Wikipedia article</a> about it); the Nazi movement was not known to Glass (at least, not in 1919-20, if ever.)
</p>
