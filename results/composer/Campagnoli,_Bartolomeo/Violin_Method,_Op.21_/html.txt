<dl><dd>Part 1. Elements of music, principles of intonation, bowing. Studies 1-73.
</dd><dd>Part 2. Double-stop exercises, chords, arpeggios, ornamentation, trill. Studies 74-140.
</dd><dd>Part 3. The 7 principal positions, rules for diminution and ornaments in Adagios, bowing patterns. Studies 141-197.
</dd><dd>Part 4. Art of playing 'una corde', enharmonics and chromatics, harmonics, exercises to overcome all difficulties, scordatura. Studies 198-250.
</dd><dd>Part 5. Accompanying text.
</dd></dl>
