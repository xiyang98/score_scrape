<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Movement indications differ in the various ms-copy sources (see RISM above.) HasB as noted @ RISM refers to the Bassani worklist of Haselbach (Haselbach, Richard, 1955, Zürich dissertation, <i>Giovanni Battista Bassani. Werkkatalog, Biographie und künstlerische Würdigung mit besonderer Berücksichtigung der konzertierenden kirchlichen Vokalmusik</i>.)
</p><p>Also called Sinfonia according to RISM. (HasB 5/1 might mean that this was Bassani's Opus 5 no.1? Bassani's Opus 5 was indeed a set of 12 Sonate a tre, republished in 1990..., poss. first published "Amsterdam&#160;: Chez Estienne Roger, [1710?]" See e.g. <span class="plainlinks"><a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://www.worldcat.org/oclc/35676248">WorldCat</a></span>.)
</p>
