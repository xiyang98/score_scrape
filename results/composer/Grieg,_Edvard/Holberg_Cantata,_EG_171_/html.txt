<p>Premiere of concert contained also premiere of the string orchestra version of the From Holberg's Time suite.
</p><p>Wikipedia gives mixed chorus, not men's chorus but seems here to be mistaken.
</p><p>Published in 1972 in a book "Four unknown cantatas by Grieg" but it seems not earlier.
</p>
