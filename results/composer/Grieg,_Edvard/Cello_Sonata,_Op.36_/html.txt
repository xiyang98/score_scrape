<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Performed in Weimar, late May 1884; perhaps earlier?
</p><p>Date 1882 from manuscript, though dating cannot always be certain even from autograph manuscript (e.g. see Hiller E minor symphony op67, whose surviving manuscript has a date clearly written at a later date - post-publication even, in that case)&#160;; publication date <span class="plainlinks"><a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://anno.onb.ac.at/cgi-content/anno-buch?apm=0&amp;aid=1000001&amp;bd=0001883&amp;teil=0203&amp;seite=00000250&amp;zoom=1">Hofmeister's <i>Monatsbericht</i> (1883), p.250</a></span> (possibly published elsewhere earlier).
</p>
