<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Please note that due to a repeated «musicological» confusion, the real name of this composer is CHARPENTIER, as clearly written on each and every score published during his lifetime, never «Beauvarlet-Charpentier»; however, his son Jacques-Marie added «Beauvarlet» to his own name probably to avoid being confused with his father. P.G.
</p>
