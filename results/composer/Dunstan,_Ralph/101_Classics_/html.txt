<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>An Album of Favorites from the uploader's family archives&#160;: short and varied pieces, of various difficulty, guaranteed to dispel or alleviate the boredom of a rainy Sunday in Great Britain. The uploader, when young and full of insouciance, used it as a relief from the lengthier works imposed upon him.
</p>
