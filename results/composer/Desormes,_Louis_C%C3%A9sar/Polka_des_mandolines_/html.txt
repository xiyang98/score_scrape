<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Actually, appears in the same issue of HMB (December 1878) released for string orchestra, then pf4h, -then- piano, all for, not 'arranged' for, so changing instr. etc. accordingly. (It was later arranged for violin and piano in 1903 according to HMB for February of that year, however.)-Schissel
</p>
