<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>This song was originally published as no 3 of Three Baritone Songs. The other songs from the set ("Come to the Woods" and "My Lady") were rapidly eclipsed by the popularity of Glorious Devon and have been all but forgotten.
</p>
