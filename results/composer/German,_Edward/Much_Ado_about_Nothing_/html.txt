<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>According to Grove Music, this was for orchestra ("for orch unless otherwise stated", with nothing stated otherwise). However, there is also a comment for all of German's incidental music that where it was published, it was in part only. Therefore, getting an overall original instrumentation will be very difficult.
</p>
