<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Included in the category <a href="/wiki/Category:Intermediate_Works_for_Violin" title="Category:Intermediate Works for Violin"><i>Intermediate Works for Violin</i></a>.<br />The violin part(s) is in 1st to 3rd position. Many of the violin parts in this series can be played in the 1st to 3rd position. One movement, the Corrente of No.3, can be played in 1st position.<br />
<a href="/wiki/Kammer-Sonaten_(Moffat,_Alfred)" title="Kammer-Sonaten (Moffat, Alfred)">Kammer-Sonaten</a> is a similar series of Baroque solo sonatas, for violin, arranged by Moffat and published by Schott.<br /><a href="/wiki/Oeuvres_d%27auteurs_anciens" title="Oeuvres d'auteurs anciens">Oeuvres d'auteurs anciens</a> is a similar series for both violin and cello, edited by <a href="/wiki/Category:Salmon,_Joseph" title="Category:Salmon, Joseph">Joseph Salmon</a> (1864-1943) and published by Ricordi ca. 1920.<br /><a href="/wiki/Cello_Library" title="Cello Library">Cello Library</a> is a long series with many editors includig Moffat, published from 1890-1930 by Schott.
