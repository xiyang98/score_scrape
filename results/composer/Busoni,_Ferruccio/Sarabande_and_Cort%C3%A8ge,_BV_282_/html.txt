<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Studies (for/from the incomplete opera "Doctor Faust") is used here more in the sense of "sketches" than studies as in "etudes", actually.
</p><p>The list given at <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Elegies_(Busoni)" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Elegies (Busoni)">Wikipedia</a> does not quite match: Elegy no.5 is "Die Nächtlichen (Walzer)" and Elegy no.6 is "Erscheinung (Notturno)", manuscripts @ Busoni Archive nos. 237-8, published 1908. However, Busoni, like Liszt and others in their tradition, was forever revising and reconsidering, so...&#160;?.
</p>
