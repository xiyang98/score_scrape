<p><i>Orchestra</i>: 3 flutes (piccolo), 3 oboes (English horn), 3 clarinets (bass clarinet), 3 bassoons (contrabassoon); 4 horns, 4 trumpets, 3 trombones, tuba; tympani, percussion (glockenspiel, triangle, tambourine, covered drum, bass drum, tam-tam); 2 harps; strings
</p>
</td></tr></table>
</div>
<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>According to <a href="/wiki/Bibliography:Busoni,_Ferruccio" title="Bibliography:Busoni, Ferruccio">Beaumont (1985)</a>, p. 85, the final 9-movement version with "Verzweiflung und Ergebung" and "Altoums Warnung" is the "definitive version" of the <i>Turandot Suite</i>.
</p>
