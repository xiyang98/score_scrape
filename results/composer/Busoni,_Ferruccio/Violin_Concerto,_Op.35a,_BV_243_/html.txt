<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>In one movement, though what feels very much like a second movement starts at G a little after "Poco agitato". Allmusic.com considers a "second movement" to start at Quasi andante and a third at "Allegro impetuoso". (No attempt has been made to include <i>every</i> tempo change in this mercurial work, just perhaps the major ones.) - Schissel
</p>
