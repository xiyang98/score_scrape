<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<h3> <span class="mw-headline" id="Publication_history">Publication history</span></h3>
<ul><li>BV 256 Fantasia contrappuntistica (edizione definitiva):
</li></ul>
<dl><dd>1) Title: Fantasia contrappuntistica. Preludio al Corale "Gloria al Signore nei Cieli" e Fuga a quattro obbligati sopra un frammento di Bach. Compilita per il Pianoforte. (Edizione definitiva)
<dl><dd>Dated: (Giugno 1910).
</dd><dd>Leipzig: <a href="/wiki/Breitkopf_%26_H%C3%A4rtel" title="Breitkopf &amp; Härtel" class="mw-redirect">Breitkopf &amp; Härtel</a>, Copyright 1910, cat.no. V.A. 3491 (45 pages)
</dd><dd>Note: The plan of the work "Distribuzione dell'opera" is on the final unpaginated page.
</dd><dd>Ref: <a href="#Kindermann_.281980.29"> Kindermann</a>, pp. 245-250.
</dd></dl>
</dd><dd>2) Title: Fantasia contrappuntistica
<dl><dd>Dedication: An Wilhelm Middelschulte, Meister des Kontrapunktes.
</dd><dd>In: Bach-Busoni Complete Edition, Vol. IV, pp. 82-123 (42 pages); pp. 125-127 (Appendix) (3 pages)
</dd><dd>Leipzig: Breitkopf &amp; Härtel, [1916], cat. no. BB IV.
</dd><dd>Reprint: Leipzig: Breitkopf &amp; Härtel, [1920], cat. no. BB IV.
</dd><dd>Note: The Appendix contains music cut from Fuga IV and still present at this point in the first edition, which Busoni decided to omit for public performance. 
</dd><dd>Ref: <a href="#Kindermann_.281980.29">Kindermann</a>, pp. 245-250.
</dd></dl>
</dd><dd>3) Title: Fantasia contrappuntistica
<dl><dd>Dedication: An <a href="/wiki/Category:Middelschulte,_Wilhelm" title="Category:Middelschulte, Wilhelm">Wilhelm Middelschulte</a>, Meister des Kontrapunktes.
</dd><dd>Wiesbaden: Breitkopf &amp; Härtel, [1983] (Roberge), cat. no. Edition Breitkopf 3491, pp. 1-43; pp. 44-45 (Appendix)
</dd><dd>Note: The Appendix contains music cut from Fuga IV (p. 37) and still present at this point in the first edition, which Busoni decided to omit for public performance. 
</dd><dd>Ref: Image:Busoni fc.pdf (14:08, 2 September 2008)
</dd><dd>Ref: <a href="#Roberge_.281991.29">Roberge</a>, p. 35.
</dd></dl>
</dd></dl>
<ul><li>BV 256a Fantasia contrappuntistica (edizione minore):
</li></ul>
<dl><dd>1) Title: Choral-Vorspiel und Fuge über ein Bachsches Fragment (Der "Fantasia Contrappuntistica" kleine Ausgabe). Preludio al Corale e Fuga sopra un frammento di Bach (Edizione minore della "Fantasia Contrappuntistica")
<dl><dd>Dedication: gewidmet Richard Buhlig.
</dd><dd>Leipzig: Breitkopf &amp; Härtel, Copyright 1912, cat.no. V.A. 3829 (21 pages)
</dd><dd>Note: This edition contains a preface by Busoni.
</dd></dl>
</dd><dd>2) Title: Choral-Vorspiel und Fuge über ein Bachsches Fragment (Der "Fantasia Contrappuntistica" kleine Ausgabe). Preludio al Corale e Fuga sopra un frammento di Bach (Edizione minore della "Fantasia Contrappuntistica")
<dl><dd>In: Bach-Busoni Complete Edition Vol. IV, pp. 61-79.
</dd><dd>Dedication: An Richard Buhlig.
</dd><dd>Leipzig: Breitkopf &amp; Härtel, [1916], cat. no. BB IV.
</dd><dd>Note: This edition contains a foreword.
</dd><dd>Reprint: Leipzig: <a href="/wiki/Breitkopf_%26_H%C3%A4rtel" title="Breitkopf &amp; Härtel" class="mw-redirect">Breitkopf &amp; Härtel</a>, [1920], cat. no. BB IV.
</dd><dd>Ref: <a href="#Kindermann_.281980.29"> Kindermann</a>, pp. 250-251.
</dd></dl>
</dd></dl>
<ul><li>BV 256b Fantasia contrappuntistica (for two pianos)
</li></ul>
<dl><dd>1) Title: Fantasia contrappuntistica. Choral-Variationen über "Ehre sei Gott in der Höhe" gefolgt von einer Quadrupel-Fuge über einBachsches Fragment für zwei Klaviere
<dl><dd>Dedication: An das Künstler- und Freundespaar Kwast-Hodapp. (to to the artistic "pair" James Kwast, and his wife Frieda Kwast-Hodapp.)
</dd><dd>Leipzig: Breitkopf &amp; Härtel, Copyright 1922, cat.no. E.B. 5196, plate no. 28713 (68 pages)
</dd><dd>Notes:
<dl><dd>1) According to a later entry in Schnapp's Busoni-Archive catalog, the now-lost autograph had the following date at the end: "Berlin, 3. Juli 1921"
</dd><dd>2) The Busoni-Archive of the Berlin State Library (Busoni-Archive No. 356) preserves a proof copy with Busoni's handwritten corrections.
</dd><dd>3) On the back of the title page is a plan of the work: "A. Analytischer 1. Choral-Variationen (Einleitung -  Choral und Variationen - Übergang). 2. Fuga I. 3. Fuga II. 4. Fuga III. 5. Intermezzo. 6. Variato I. 7. Variato II. 8. Variato III. 9. Cadenza. 10. Fuga IV. 11. Corale. 12. Stretta. - B. Architektonischer: Zeichnung in Form einer Gebäudefassade."
</dd><dd>4) According to the B&amp;H Busoni catalog this edition appeared in 1921.
</dd></dl>
</dd><dd>Reprints:
<dl><dd>1) Leipzig: VEB Breitkopf &amp; Härtel, [1955 or later]
</dd><dd>2) Wiesbaden: Breitkopf &amp; Härtel, [1966]
</dd></dl>
</dd><dd>Ref: <a href="#Kindermann_.281980.29"> Kindermann</a>, p. 251.
</dd></dl>
</dd></dl>
