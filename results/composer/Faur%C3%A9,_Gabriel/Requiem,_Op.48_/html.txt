<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<ul><li>Tagging this work is difficult since it started life as separate movements.  However, the first multi-movement version (1887-8) already had harp, timpani, and organ along with the strings, and so the tag would be "orch".  The solo string parts are not tagged separately.<br />
</li></ul>
<h4> <span class="mw-headline" id="Composition_History">Composition History</span></h4>
<ul><li>1877 (<i>Libera me</i>, 1st version for baritone and organ)
</li><li>1887/88 (<i>Introït, Sanctus, Pie Jesu, Agnus Dei, In paradisum</i>: Fauré’s 1st version perf. 16 January 1888)
</li><li>1889 (<i>Offertoire</i>)
</li><li>1892/93 (<i>Libera me</i>, 2nd version for baritone, choir, orchestra and organ)
</li><li>1893 (revisions and extra instrumentation: Fauré’s 2nd version perf. January 1893)
</li><li>1900 (published version, re-orchestrated by Jean Roger-Ducasse)
</li></ul>
<h4> <span class="mw-headline" id="Publication_History">Publication History</span></h4>
<ul><li>Paris: J. Hamelle, n.d. (1900), ed. Jean Roger-Ducasse; full score, vocal score J. 4531 H.
</li></ul>
