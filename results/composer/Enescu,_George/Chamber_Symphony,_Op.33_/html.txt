<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>1950s on- modern, iirc. Composition concluded 1954 May 28 (see <span class="plainlinks"><a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://gallica.bnf.fr/ark:/12148/cb39728461g/PUBLIC">BNF entry</a></span>.) As with many other chamber symphonies (Schoenberg, etc.) instruments are <i>all</i> arguably treated more as soloists than "in the mass" (one reason why all the performers in the 1999 concert are listed.) 
</p><p>Final work (or complete work?) apparently? (though the two unfinished symphonies, 4 and 5, both predate this- still, always possible there are unfinished works he was working on after it too? Will have to find a copy of Bentoiu and some other related books...)- ES
</p>
