<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Tobacco is Hume's most famous, though now rather politically incorrect, piece. The two original prints of Captaine Hume are available <a href="/wiki/The_First_Part_of_Ayres,_French,_Pollish_and_Others_(Hume,_Tobias)" title="The First Part of Ayres, French, Pollish and Others (Hume, Tobias)">collection page</a>, but you will find the modern notation much easier to read.
</p>
