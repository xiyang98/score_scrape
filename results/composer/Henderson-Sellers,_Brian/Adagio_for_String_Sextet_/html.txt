<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Originally written (in 1971) as the slow movement to an as yet unpublished sonatina for solo piano, I rescored this for string sextet soon thereafter. 
The work was then rescored in 2015-16 for both clarinet choir and for wind band(both transposed into G minor).
</p>
