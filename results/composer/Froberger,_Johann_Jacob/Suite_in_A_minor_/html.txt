<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>According to Grove Music, Froberger's published output was entirely keyboard music, so these are most likely arrangements.  Until the originals are found, this page cannot be tagged.
</p>
<dl><dd>Partial solution? - In part at least- I can't seem to <i>read</i> the above... an arrangement of the well-known Strassbourg (that is, from the Strassbourger manuscript - now @ Dresden manuscript 1-T-595) A-minor suite, Adler 15, recorded in 1999 by Ludger Rmy for cpo (for example) (on a 2-CD set of 14 keyboard suites from/in aforesaid manuscript, by Froberger)- see e.g. <span class="plainlinks"><a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="https://opac.rism.info/metaopac/search?q=rism211020567&amp;db=251&amp;View=rism&amp;Language=en">Dresden 1-T-595, A minor suite, copy (ca.1675) copy by Bulyovszky</a></span>  - ES
