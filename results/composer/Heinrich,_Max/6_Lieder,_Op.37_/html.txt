<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>The identity of F.E. Toennis is very elusive.  These songs appear to be the only works referenced under that name in Worldcat.  There was a German philosopher and sociologist named <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ferdinand_T%C3%B6nnies" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Ferdinand Tönnies">Ferdinand Tönnies</a> (1855–1936) who seems unlikely to have been the author.  There was also an F.E. Toennies active ca.1900-1920 in New York City as a manufacturer of soap and perfumes who also seems an unlikely candidate.
</p>
