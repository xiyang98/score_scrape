<h3> <span class="mw-headline" id="Instrumentation">Instrumentation</span></h3>
<p>The cover page to the Muzyka score gives the general instrumentation, and then separate instrumentations for the Scenas (since there are no strings involved, these are possibly on-stage parts?):
</p>

</li></ul>
<dl><dd>piccolo, 2 flutes, oboe, english horn, 3 clarinets in B<span class="music-symbol" style="font-family: Arial Unicode MS, Lucida Sans Unicode; font-size:110%">&#x266d;</span> and A, 2 bassoons, 4 horns in F, 2 trumpets in B<span class="music-symbol" style="font-family: Arial Unicode MS, Lucida Sans Unicode; font-size:110%">&#x266d;</span> A E<span class="music-symbol" style="font-family: Arial Unicode MS, Lucida Sans Unicode; font-size:110%">&#x266d;</span> and F, 3 trombones, tuba, timpani, triangle, drum, cymbals, bass drum, tamtam, harp, strings
</dd></dl>
<ul><li>I Atto
</li></ul>
<dl><dd>2 piccolos, 4 flutes, 4 oboes, 4 clarinets in B<span class="music-symbol" style="font-family: Arial Unicode MS, Lucida Sans Unicode; font-size:110%">&#x266d;</span>, 4 bassoons, 4 horns in F, 2 trumpets in F, 3 trombones, tuba, triangle, drum, cymbals, bass drum
</dd></dl>
<ul><li>IV Atto
</li></ul>
<dl><dd>flute, english horn, 2 clarinets in A, 2 bassoons, harp
</dd></dl>
<ul><li>V Atto
</li></ul>
<dl><dd>3 trumpets in B<span class="music-symbol" style="font-family: Arial Unicode MS, Lucida Sans Unicode; font-size:110%">&#x266d;</span> and E<span class="music-symbol" style="font-family: Arial Unicode MS, Lucida Sans Unicode; font-size:110%">&#x266d;</span>, 3 horns in E<span class="music-symbol" style="font-family: Arial Unicode MS, Lucida Sans Unicode; font-size:110%">&#x266d;</span>, alto flugelhorn in E<span class="music-symbol" style="font-family: Arial Unicode MS, Lucida Sans Unicode; font-size:110%">&#x266d;</span>, 2 tenor flugelhorns in B<span class="music-symbol" style="font-family: Arial Unicode MS, Lucida Sans Unicode; font-size:110%">&#x266d;</span>, 2 bass flugelhorns, contrabass flugelhorn, triangle, drum, cymbals, bass drum
