<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>A long time Barrios used "Souvenir d'un Rêve" as the title, then (1930) he called it "Un Sueño en la Floresta". Recorded by composer (Odeon, 1928).
