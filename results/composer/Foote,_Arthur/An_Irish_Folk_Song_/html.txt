<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>The song was originally published for soprano or tenor in G minor in 1894.  Transcriptions/arrangements in the following forms were published:
</p>
<dl><dd><dl><dd>for string orchestra
</dd><dd>for mezzo-soprano or baritone in F minor
</dd><dd>for alto or bass in E minor
</dd><dd>with violin obbligato for soprano or tenor in G minor
</dd><dd>with violin obbligato for mezzo-soprano or baritone in F minor
</dd><dd>with violin obbligato for alto or bass in E minor
</dd><dd>with violin and violincello in E minor
</dd><dd>piano solo
</dd></dl>
</dd></dl>
