<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Contains some early Beethoven compositions, including "Schilderung eines Mädchen," written at the age of 11. <br />Most represented composers are Schmittbaur, Rosetti and Christmann<br />
Includes works by following composers unidentified or not yet on IMSLP:
</p>
<ul><li>Möller/Moeller
</li><li>Köhler/Koehler (possibly Gottlieb Heinrich)
</li><li>Weber
</li><li>Walther (probably Thomas Christian Walter)
</li><li>Sch...rt (Schobert? Schubart is named in full later)
</li><li>Schu..rt
</li><li>Kirchner
</li><li>Lorenz (maybe Johann Hendrik Lorentz?)
</li><li>Eilenstein
</li><li>Lauffenmaier
</li><li>Kapellmeister Schönfeld
</li><li>Reinhardt
</li><li>Sauerbrey
</li><li>Stelzer
</li><li>C. G. Weber 
</li><li>Metzger (Franz Mezger? or Johann Georg Mezger?)
</li><li>Enslin
</li><li>Rheinhart
</li><li>Dr. Pregel
</li><li>Segmüller/Stegmüller (possibly Stegmayer?)
</li><li>Stükelberger
</li><li>Mörschel
</li><li>Caroline, Prinzessin von Sachsen
</li><li>J. C. Rudolph
</li><li>Siedler
</li><li>Wolf (Ernst Wilhelm or Georg Friedrich? or Christian Michael Wolff?)
</li><li>Steffan (presumably Josef Antonín Štěpán)
</li></ul>
