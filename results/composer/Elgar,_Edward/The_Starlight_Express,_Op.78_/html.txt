<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<ul><li>According to the Elgar Society, this is "Incidental music to the play by Violet Pearn, based on the play <i>A Prisoner in Fairyland</i> by Algernon Blackwood, comprising orchestral entr'actes and songs for soprano and baritone soloists".  Therefore, it is a little unclear whether Elgar took the words to the songs from Pearn's text or Blackwood's.
</li></ul>
