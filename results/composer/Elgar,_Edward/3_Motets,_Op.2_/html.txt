<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<ul><li>The three motets were composed in 1887.  No.1 was originally conceived as Pie Jesu and revised in 1902 as Ave Verum.  Nos.2 &amp; 3 were revised in 1907. I see no evidence in Worldcat of publication prior to the revised versions.
</li></ul>
