<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>While this song is traditionally attributed to Giuseppe Giordani, scholar John Glen Paton proposes that it is actually by Tommaso Giordani in his book 26 Italian Songs And Arias: An Authoritative Edition Based On Authentic Sources. <br /> The Nanki Library copy is bound in a volume of publications by <b>Tommaso</b> Giordani, which may reinforce this notion.
</p>
