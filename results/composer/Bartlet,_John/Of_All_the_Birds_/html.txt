<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>First published in <i>A booke of Ayres: 1606</i>.  According to WorldCat, the full subtitle is "with a triplicitie of musicke, where of the first part is for the lute or orpharion, and the viole de gambo and 4 partes to sing, the second part is for 2 trebles to sing to the lute and viole, the third part is for the lute and one voyce, and the viole de gambo".
</p>
