<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>According to Grove, the op.15b is a string quartet.
</p><p>From Càceres' worklist PDF: opus 15 is a Phantasiestück, 1856 (composition and publication years). opus 15a is this octet, composed 1849/50, published 1877. opus 15b is quartet no.3, composed by April 16 1851, published 1877.
</p>
