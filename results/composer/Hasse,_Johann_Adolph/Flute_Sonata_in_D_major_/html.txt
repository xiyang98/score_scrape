<th>Misc. Notes
</th>
<td>Transposition of original up a minor third for Recorder. Second movement needed a bit of reworking to get some notes into the Recorder in F's range and also some phrases were lowered an octave where appropriate as the range of this piece as a pure transposition is too high for my taste. Gets a bit boring with everything always in the high register. Also added an alternate third movement with embellishments on the second repeat of the first section. XML files posted on scores page if others can make their own versions if they do not like this.
</td></tr>

<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<h4> <span class="mw-headline" id="About_the_Kram_Edition">About the Kram Edition</span></h4>
<p>Too nice piece to just sit in manuscript. Edited it and added a realized continuo. Only a few measures of the first movement had a figured bass so I did not notate one. Recorder transcription posted on the Arrangements and Transcriptions tab. Complete Parts as separate files for each movement zipped up. Created in Notion which is not he best for engraving, but the parts are perfectly readable. XML also posted for those who want to make their own editions or continuo modifications.
</p>
