<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>This is the only in Raverii's collection to specify specific instrumentation: 8 trombones. Such instructions were relatively rare for the period. The piece is not especially idiomatic to trombones, and can be easily played on 8 bass instruments, or even a combination of mostly bass with a few tenor instruments.
</p><p>The parts are labeled for two choirs but do not follow the typical polychoral practice of echo and imitation.
</p>
