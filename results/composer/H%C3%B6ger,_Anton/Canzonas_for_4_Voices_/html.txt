<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>"Canzona in B flat major": from Johann Woltz Tabulatur, 1617, no.8.
"Canzona in F minor (No.1)": from Vienna MS XIV 714 (Edited in "Canzoni d'intavolature d'organo" / Claudio Merulo&#160;; edited by Walker Cunningham and Charles McDermott (Madison&#160;: A-R Editions, c1992)).
"Canzona in F minor (No.2)": Canzon quinta a4 (Alessandro Raverii, Venice, 1608).
"Canzona in G minor": from Basel ms., 49 and 50 (Edited in "Canzoni d'intavolature d'organo" / Claudio Merulo&#160;; edited by Walker Cunningham and Charles McDermott (Madison&#160;: A-R Editions, c1992)).
