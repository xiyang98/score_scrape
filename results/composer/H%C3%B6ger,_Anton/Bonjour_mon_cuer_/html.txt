<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<ul><li>It seems the copyist for the tablature edition of 1583 stopped at the bottom of folio 131b just before an A over an A major chord, and when he came back for the rest of the song, beginning a new page (fol. 132), he inadvertendly jumped 14 beats to a similar A (superius) note but over a D major chord; so this whole passage («<i>-temps, ma douce fleur nouvelle, mon doux plaisir, ma...</i>») is missing.
</li><li>As with the dances in the <i>Ein Schön Nutz . . .</i> collection, there is the possibility that Paix simply arranged the chanson, and that this should be listed as an arrangement, which would also leave tagging as subject to correction.  See Comments for <a href="/wiki/Ungarescha_and_Saltarello_(Paix,_Jakob)" title="Ungarescha and Saltarello (Paix, Jakob)">Ungarescha and Saltarello (Paix, Jakob)</a>, for the equivalent notes to the dances from this collection.
</li></ul>
