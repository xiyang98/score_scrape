<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<ul><li>None of those two copies bears any original title; the set looks like a typical pair of keyboard sonatas, suitable for the organ as well as the harpsichord.
</li><li>Soler's output was mainly sonatas, but according to Grove Music, none exist in autograph manuscripts.
</li></ul>
