<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<ul><li>According to one sources, the first part of this is for 4 voices, and the remaining four sections are for seven voices.  Accordingly, it is tagged for undetermined instrumentation until an original can be checked.
</li><li>Possibly, the original was published with one of Striggio's other madrigal "entertainments" (sometimes called madrigal comedies), <i>Il cicalamento delle donne al bucato et la caccia </i>.
</li></ul>
