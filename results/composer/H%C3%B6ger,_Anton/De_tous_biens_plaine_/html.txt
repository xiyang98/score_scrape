<th>Extra Information

<td>No less than two dozen fifteenth century sources contain Ghizeghem's setting of this chanson. Many other composers of the period made arrangements of the tune, including <a href="/wiki/Category:Josquin_Desprez" title="Category:Josquin Desprez">Josquin Desprez</a> produced 2 settings: one in 3 parts the other in 4.

</dd></dl>

</li></ul>
