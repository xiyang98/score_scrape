<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<ul><li>Grove Music lists the date as 1550, though it is unclear whether that is composition or publication.  It exists in <i>Libro I</i> of <i>Tutti i Madrigali di Cipriano di Rore a Quattro Voci</i> published in 1577 by Gardano. It's also present in the 1569 Gardano edition reprint of de Rore's <i>Il primo libro de madrigali a 4 voci</i>.
</li></ul>
