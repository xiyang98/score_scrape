<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<ul><li>Probably from the Canzoni of 1605 which, according to Grove Music, includes 17 works for four parts (one by Gasparo Costa) and four vocal and instrumental pieces on sacred texts for eight parts in two choirs. The titles of each song refer to the members of the academy of Prospero Lombardo.
</li></ul>
