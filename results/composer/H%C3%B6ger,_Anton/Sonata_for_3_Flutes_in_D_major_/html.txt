<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>In Keiser's work list, Grove Music lists a Concerto in D for flute and strings, a Sonata in D for 2 flutes and bc, and a set of Sonatas for flute, violin, and bc, but not a Sonata for 3 flutes, though there is a citation that says "Other works, mentioned by Mattheson, lost".  Further, Grove Music lists the Italian form of his first name as Rinardo, not Rinaldo.  Given this, it is possible that this work is spurious.
</p>
