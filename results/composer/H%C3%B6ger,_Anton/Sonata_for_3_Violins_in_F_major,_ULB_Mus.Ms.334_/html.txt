<th>Misc. Notes
</th>
<td>This is a typeset of the original work, with an additional Cello part, freely composed at an early date (ca.1960) by the uploader, who felt, rightly or wrongly, as he was playing the cello at the time, that it required "beefing up". When scanning the original score, this forgotten cello manuscript fell out, and just as it was going to be trashed, the uploader rediscovered his initial intent and decided to take it up again. The original parts for 3 violins are not modified in any way nor edited, so this version can serve as the score with a non obligato bass instrument thrown in.
