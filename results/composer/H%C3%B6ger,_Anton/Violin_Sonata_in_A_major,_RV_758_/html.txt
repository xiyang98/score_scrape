<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>No.6 of the "Manchester Sonatas". (Possibly published ca.1965... definitely published in an edition by Michael Talbot for A-R Editions in 1976 after the discovery of the 12 sonatas in 1965 (there is an interesting story behind the name Manchester Sonatas...), see <span class="plainlinks"><a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://www.worldcat.org/oclc/65553605">WorldCat</a></span>. This movement exists, along with another from the sonata (the first Corrente?), in an organ transcription as part of RV 746 (withdrawn), which (I think I've read) was discovered by Malipiero among Pescetti's works (whether it was published before then I do not know.)) - Schissel
