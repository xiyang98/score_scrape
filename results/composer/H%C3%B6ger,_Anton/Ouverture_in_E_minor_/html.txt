<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>HedC after Krister Hede's typescript work <i>Fortunato Chelleri och Sverige</i> (Uppsala, 1976). The mss copies judging from RISM have the same first movement incipit but are not identical with the work here in movement layout or with each other, it seems (hence HedC O19a, b, d, etc.)
</p>
