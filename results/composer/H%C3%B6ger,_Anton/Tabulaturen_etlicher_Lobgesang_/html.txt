<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<ul><li>According to Grove Music, "The book contains nine works for organ, in three to five parts, 12 lute pieces with ‘zwo stimmen zu zwicken und ein zu singen’, and three works for lute with ‘drei stimmen zu zwicken’. Among the organ works a five-part Salve regina is outstanding".  The Salve Regina is textless.
</li><li>It is unclear how the two- and three-voice parts are to be "adapted" ("zwicken").
</li></ul>
