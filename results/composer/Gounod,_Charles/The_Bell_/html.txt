<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Though BnF has an autograph manuscript that has the accompaniment as orchestral, Grove Music says the accompaniment was piano.  BnF also says this was in a collection of <i>6 New Part Songs</i>, second series, no.3.  It is unknown whether the orchestral version pre-dates the collection, or even if that collection had piano accompaniment (though it probably did).  Tagged for both for now, and subject to correction.
</p>
