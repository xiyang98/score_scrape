<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>The first publisher Simrock's editor in chief Robert Keller, in a letter to the composer of 26 May 1879, criticized the <i>schereiende und unaufgeloste Dizssonanzen</i> (shrill and unresolved dissonances) of the first movement, in response to which Dvorak shortened the exposition by 45 measures and cut 20 measures from the development.
