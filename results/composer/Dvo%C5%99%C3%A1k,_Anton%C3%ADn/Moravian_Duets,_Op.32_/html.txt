<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<ul><li>B.50 of 1875/76, Op.20, is the "first series". The composer released 4 series of Moravian Duets, it seems, and a further work by that title for a cappella chorus.
</li></ul>
<p>However, B.60 has only 5 pieces. The other works listed here are parts of <i>other</i> sets of Moravian duets by the composer (see <a href="/wiki/List_of_works_by_Anton%C3%ADn_Dvo%C5%99%C3%A1k" title="List of works by Antonín Dvořák">List of works by Antonín Dvořák</a>.)
</p>
<dl><dd>However, Op.32 has the 14 pieces listed here. So, Instead of splitting things (which nobody has time to do), why not just leave this as a <i>collection</i> page (with double-billing) and set up separate pages for B.60, B.62, et al as they arrive? <a href="/wiki/User:Carolus" title="User:Carolus">Carolus</a>
</dd></dl>
