<th><span class="mh555">Movements/Sections</span><span class="ms555">Mov'ts/Sec's</span>
</th>
<td>18 songs
<dl><dd>1. Vy vrouci pisne spejte (You ardent songs, go forth through the night)
</dd><dd>2. V te sladke moci oci tvych (In that sweet power of your eyes)
</dd><dd>3. V tak mnohem srdci mrtvo jest (So many a heart is as though dead)
</dd><dd>4. O duse drama jedinka (Oh dear soul, the only one)
</dd><dd>5. O byl to krasny zlaty sen (Oh, it was a lovely, golden dream)
</dd><dd>6. Ja vim, ze v slake nadeji (I know that in sweet hope)
</dd><dd>7. O zlata ruze spaniel (Oh golden rose, fair)
</dd><dd>8. O nasi lasce… (Oh, that longed-for happiness does not bloom for our love)
</dd><dd>9. Kol domu se ted potacim (Around the house now I stagger)
</dd><dd>10. Mne casto tyra pochyba (Doubt often torments me)
</dd><dd>11. Me srdce casto v boleti (My heart often broods in pain)
</dd><dd>12. Zde hledim na ten dray list (Here I look upon this dear letter)
</dd><dd>13. Na horach ticho (On the mountains quiet)
</dd><dd>14. Zde v lese u potoka (Here in the forest by a brook)
</dd><dd>15. Mou celou dusk (Pensively through my whole soul)
</dd><dd>16. Tam stojí stara skala (There stands an old crag)
</dd><dd>17. Nad krajem vevodi leaky spanek (Over the countryside reigns a light sleep)
</dd><dd>18. Ty se ptas, proc moue zpevy (You ask why my songs)
</dd></dl>
</td></tr>
