<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<ul><li>Originally published as piano Sonata, Op.11.
</li></ul>
<ul><li>Do we know at least whether the _composer_ called it a toccata or a sonata on any autograph ms that might possibly have survived (rare, in Clementi&#8217;s case, I know, with the conflagration carried on by his relatives, but there are some)? That's quite important - Schissel (see RISM link for some information about a manuscript copy)
</li><li> I hope I found the answer. In the Litolff collection the Toccata comes at the end of the well known Sonata Op.47 No.2 and the present Sonata shows up as Sonata Op.20.
</li></ul>
