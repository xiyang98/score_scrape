<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Believed to have been composed around the first decade of the 19th century, but held back until 1821. Published in 1821 by <a href="/wiki/Naderman" title="Naderman">Naderman</a> (among others?). (Publication noted in the <i><a href="/wiki/Bibliographie_de_la_France" title="Bibliographie de la France">Bibliographie de la France</a></i>&#160;1e série, 10e année (24e de la collection), Nº 42, 26 octobre 1821, p.578, r.258.)
</p>
