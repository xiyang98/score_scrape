<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>"Babes in Toyland" features some of Herbert's most famous songs - among them "Toyland", "March of the Toys", "Go To Sleep, Slumber Deep", and "I Can't Do The Sum". Songs "Toyland" and "March of the Toys" occasionally show up on Christmas compilations.
</p>
