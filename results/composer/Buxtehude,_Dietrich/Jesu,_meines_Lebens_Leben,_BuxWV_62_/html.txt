<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<ul><li>Grove Music does not list the flute part, although <span class="plainlinks"><a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="https://opac.rism.info/search?id=190006930&amp;Language=en">RISM</a></span> does.  However, the RISM entry says "C. A. T. B. Con 5 instromenti", and the string parts + continuo add up to 5 without the flute part.  The RISM entry also says the copyist was "Schreiber: Düben, Gustav &lt;1628c-1690&gt;", so this is not an autograph manuscript or an official first edition.  On the other hand, Grove Music says there are 2 versions, but does not elaborate on what the differences are.  Since the flute part anyway doubles 1st violin for the entire piece, it is left out of the tag.
</li><li>After the Sinfonia, the Aria is composed over a chaconne bass.
</li></ul>
