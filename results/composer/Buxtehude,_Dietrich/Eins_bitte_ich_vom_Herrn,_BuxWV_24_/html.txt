<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Quantz 1752, says the German Violon is an instrument of five or six strings. The Violon is an 8-foot (not octavating) bass viola da gamba of large proportions. In modern terminology it is usually referred to as "G violone".
</p><p>Soprano I &amp; II and alto voices contain solo movements (hence the 3 voice + choir option). "Flute" parts double violins at certain points and are not mentioned in the original tablature score. Probably not original.
</p>
