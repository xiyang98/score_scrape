<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>The full score appears to show 2 flutes and a piccolo (without a player doubling). The orchestra parts include 3 flutes with the third corresponding to the piccolo as seen in the full score.
</p><p>Key becomes fairly clearly A-flat major (and ends there as well) after a brief tonally-unsettled introduction.
</p>
