<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<ul><li><i>The Battell</i>, BK 94 seems to have been inspired by <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Image_of_Irelande,_with_a_Discoverie_of_Woodkarne" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:The Image of Irelande, with a Discoverie of Woodkarne"><i>The Image of Irelande, with a Discoverie of Woodkarne</i></a> a series of engravings by <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/John_Derricke" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:John Derricke">John Derricke</a> published in 1581 depicting <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Henry_Sidney" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Henry Sidney">Henry Sidney's</a> campaign of 1578 against Turlough Luineach O'Neill, King of Tyrone. In numerous arrangements and recordings, the adjacent works found in the 1591 <i>My Ladye Nevells Booke</i> collection - <i>The Marche before the Battell</i>, BK 93 (No.3) and <i>The Galliard for the Victorie</i>, BK 95 (No.5) are included to form a "Battle Suite".
</li></ul>
