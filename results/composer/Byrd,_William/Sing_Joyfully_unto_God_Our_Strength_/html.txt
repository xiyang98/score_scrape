<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<h3> <span class="mw-headline" id="English_Text">English Text</span></h3>
<p>Sing joyfully to God our strength; sing loud unto the God of Jacob!<br />
Take the song, bring forth the timbrel, the pleasant harp, and the viol.<br />
Blow the trumpet in the new moon, even in the time appointed, and at our feast day.<br />
For this is a statute for Israel, and a law of the God of Jacob.
</p>
<h3> <span class="mw-headline" id="Danish_translation">Danish translation</span></h3>
<p>Bryd ud i jubel for Gud, vor styrke, bryd ud i fryderåb for Jakobs Gud!<br />
Istem sangen, lad pauken lyde, den dejlige citer og harpen.<br />
Stød i vædderhornet på nymånedagen og på vor festdag ved fuldmåne.<br />
Det er en lov i Israel, det er et bud fra Jakobs Gud.
</p>
