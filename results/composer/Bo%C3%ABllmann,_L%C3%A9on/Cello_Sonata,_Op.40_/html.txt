<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>apparently according to some reference sources, composed in 1897 (Boëllmann's last year) as well as published then though I am not sure of their evidence (his letters?...) - would also be good to find out if Delsart premiered the work for instance and with what pianist (the composer?) and when.
