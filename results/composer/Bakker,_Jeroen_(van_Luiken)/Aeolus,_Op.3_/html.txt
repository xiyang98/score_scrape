<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>This work was written as a theme for a theatrical radio drama series named 'Aeolus'. The series was cancelled before the music was recorded. There is an opening title and a closing title.
</p>
