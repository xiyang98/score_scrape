<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Grove Music describes this collection as "examples of embellished motets, madrigals and chansons by Willaert, Clemens non Papa, Crecquillon, Lasso, Rore, Striggio, Palestrina and Marenzio", which would indicate that these are not straight publications of the composers' original madrigals, but arrangements.
</p>
