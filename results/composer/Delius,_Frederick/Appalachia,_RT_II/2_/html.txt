<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>American Rhapsody is the name of the first version of Appalachia (from 1896), not the final, usually-performed one. RTii/2 is the catalog number belonging to Appalachia (from 1902), which is what's here. American Rhapsody - the first version of Appalachia - was first published after 1961, I think, and probably would not be available at Google! - Schissel
</p><p>Premiered 1904-10-15 according to <i>Delius and His Music</i> (Lee-Browne, Guinery), p.153-54.
</p>
