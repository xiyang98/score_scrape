<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>While the opera's Debussy-like qualities are sometimes remarked, Delius wrote that the first Debussy he ever heard was Pelléas in a spring 1902 performance, _after_ he'd finished composing this opera.
</p>
