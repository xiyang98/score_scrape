<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>These pieces have (per title page) been extracted from a larger work.  Per the IMSLP worklist, Op. 228 is "Technik und Melodie (Elementar-Klavierschule)" - but this does not seem to be the sort of piece one would expect to find in a work with that title.
