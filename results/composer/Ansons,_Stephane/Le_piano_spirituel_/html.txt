<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<h4> <span class="mw-headline" id="From_the_Composer">From the Composer</span></h4>
<p>This piece is part of a collection I named "The spiritual piano" as it is related to the main topics of the Gospel. I will release progressively other works from that collection. Some of them have still to be noted into Finale to have them in a clear format. I am still composing the old way: paper sheet, pen and a eraser! Enjoy music&#160;!
</p><p>This work cannot be used for commercial purpose without the agreement of the composer. Please contact me by email in this case.
</p>
<ul><li>Note on 'Dieu de vrit': "This piece uses the whole tone scale to figure out God's truth"
</li><li>Gurison - This piece uses lefthand voicings and upper structures common in modern Jazz. But this piece is a classical one not jazz.
</li><li>Revelation - I have named the final piece after the last book of the Bible. The piece makes a good usage of lefthand chord's voicings . I am pretty happy with the fact that these typical jazz chords have now joined the classical piano mainstream.
</li></ul>
