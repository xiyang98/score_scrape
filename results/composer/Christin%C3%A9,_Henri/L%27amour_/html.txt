<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>The parts include 1st and 2nd violins, viola, cello, bass, but the string parts do not include the 16-bar introduction in the piano part, and the string parts also have the subtitle "Polka-Marche d'après la Chanson de Polin, so it seems the string version is separate and "based on" the vocal/piano version.  One of the cover pages also gives options for piano solo and voice solo, and the string parts.  Tagged for all of those.
</p>
