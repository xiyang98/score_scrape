<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>D. Gallo's 12 Trio Sonatas were long mis-attributed to Pergolesi, as they were in R. Bremner's original 1780 London publication.  They are particularly well-known as part of Stravinsky's arrangements of pieces for Pulcinella.
</p>
