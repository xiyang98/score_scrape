<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Hofmeister describes the three sonatas as being in B-dur, G-dur and F-dur. As often, they have some of the keys wrong (no.3 is in D minor, very clearly just from the opening exposition of the violin part; nor is this a false entrance.)
</p><p>Republished, also by Peters, in an edition by Friedrich Hermann in the earlyish? 20th century (a publication of 6 sonatas - opp.5 and 23). The plate numbers 5902-3 indicate 1877. The Royal Dutch Library has another, Litolff, edition edited by Dello. The keys are indeed B<span class="music-symbol" style="font-family: Arial Unicode MS, Lucida Sans Unicode; font-size:110%">&#x266d;</span> major, G major, D minor (the work- this edition perhaps - can be found in Pazdírek vol.V, p.561, according to KB Netherlands&#160;; Archive.org has Pazdirek so this can probably be verified). KB NL also has the 1836 edition of all 3, with plates 2606 to 2608. - Schissel
</p>
