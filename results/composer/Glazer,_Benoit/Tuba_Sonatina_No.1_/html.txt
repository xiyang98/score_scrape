<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>A modern piece that is quite tonal, it moves quickly between keys. The first movement is fairly quick, with a slow section in the middle, and entitled: Joyous Orlando.
The second movement is slow and reflective, and is called: Love and Sadness. The third movement is called Orlando Strong and is a powerful 12/8 that tips its hat to Afro Cuban influences, mixed with more northern, Prokofiev like colors.
</p><p>I also have this piece scored for tuba, brass quintet and woodwind quintet, and I am working on a full orchestra version as well.
</p>
