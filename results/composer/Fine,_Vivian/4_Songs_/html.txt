<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<h4> <span class="mw-headline" id="Reviews">Reviews</span></h4>
<p>...astonishing, fully mature, intelligent, inspired pieces written by a 20-year-old using melodic and contrapuntal procedures very like those revealed in last Sunday’s all-Fine concert to be her present manner. The text-setting is marvelous. Only Virgil Thomson’s setting of the English language rivals it among 20th century composers. It is natural, perfectly speech-like, yet measured and expressive. 
</p>
<dl><dd><dl><dd><dl><dd>—Charles Shere, <i>The Oakland Tribune</i>, January 15, 1983
</dd></dl>
