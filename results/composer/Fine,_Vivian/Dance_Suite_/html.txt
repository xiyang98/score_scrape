<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p><i>Dance Suite</i> is an orchestral arrangement by Fine of material from two of her best-known dance compositions, <i>The Race of Life</i> (1937), and <i>Opus 51</i> (1938).
</p>
