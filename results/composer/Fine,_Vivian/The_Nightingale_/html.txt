<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<h4> <span class="mw-headline" id="Reviews">Reviews</span></h4>
<p>[The piece] is set as an extended fantasy for solo singer, accompanying herself with triangle, woodblock and cymbal….the result is eloquently moving.”
</p>
<dl><dd><dl><dd><dl><dd>—Heuwell Tircuit, <i>San Francisco Chronicle</i>
</dd></dl>
</dd></dl>
</dd></dl>
<p>Nightingale is an avian tour de force….Woodblock, triangle, suspended cymbal and voice created a nostalgic, almost mystical mood which left me wishing that the work were longer.”
</p>
<dl><dd><dl><dd><dl><dd>—Richard Capparela, <i>KITE</i>, Albany, New York
</dd></dl>
