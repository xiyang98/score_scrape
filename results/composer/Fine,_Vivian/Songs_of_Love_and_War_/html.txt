<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>Commissioned by Stephen Walt, in memory of his father.
</p><p>[After <i>Songs and Arias</i>] Fine...maintained a serious mood for her next composition, <i>Songs of Love and War</i>.... This is not a cycle, but a series of songs about death using lyrics from some of Fine's favorite sources....the music is simple and at times severe....Often Fine doubled the vocal line, frequently with the oboe, and although the ensemble is quite large, she refrained from overwriting, having sparse counterpoints and occasional percussive material highlight the text.
</p>
<dl><dd><dl><dd><dl><dd>—Heidi Von Gunden, <i>The Music of Vivian Fine</i>, Scarecrow Press, 1999
</dd></dl>
