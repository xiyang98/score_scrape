<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<p>The Idylls both have the subtitle "Founded on folk-tunes".  The first idyll uses three Sussex folk-tunes: "Dabbling in the dew", "Just as the tide was flowing" and "Henry Martin".  The last two were collected by Butterworth himself.
</p>
