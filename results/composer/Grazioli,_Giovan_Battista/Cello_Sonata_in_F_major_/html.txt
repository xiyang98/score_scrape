<h2> <span class="mw-headline" id="Misc._Comments">Misc. Comments</span></h2>
<ul><li>Though Grazioli lived until 1820, Grove Music describes him as from a "school of composers in decline" and that he "still clearly preserves the use of a basso continuo".  Not all of his instrumental works are listed, but of those that are, most involve harpsichord, except for one that is listed as "hpd/pf".  This makes it more likely that the cello sonata originally had continuo accompaniment.
</li></ul>
